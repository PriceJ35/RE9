Playable Characters

Two protagonists, two distinct play styles

👦
Grace Ashcroft
Survival Horror

FBI intelligence analyst and daughter of Alyssa Ashcroft (RE Outbreak). Lacking combat experience, Grace relies on stealth, evasion, and resource management. Her route is set in the Sanatorium with first-person perspective — hiding from monsters instead of fighting them. She can only push zombies down with physical attacks and must use the Hemolytic Syringe for permanent kills.

FBI Analyst Stealth Focus 1st Person S&S M232 B934 Pistol
💣
Leon S. Kennedy
Action Combat

The legendary RE2/RE4 protagonist returns. Leon brings full combat expertise with a versatile arsenal including the Alligator Handgun, Requiem Revolver, and Axe. He can parry melee attacks with his axe, perform stealth takedowns, and execute finishers on stunned enemies. His route features intense action combat sequences.

Complete step-by-step guide for every chapter

 Both

Final encounter with the Shadow Girl. Activate 3 power systems while evading her. Use searchlights to stun her. Open the roof for sunlight to finish her.

 Both

Escape the Titan Spider, then fight it using gas canisters. When flipped, attack the glowing weak point on its abdomen with the shotgun.

 Both

Chapter 8: Police Station — Tyrant Chase

Run from the Tyrant through the police station. Visit S.T.A.R.S. office for nostalgic callbacks (Rebecca's med kit, Jill's hat, Chris's coat).

 Leon

Parry the Tyrant's attacks with the axe (Spacebar / L1 / LB). Aim for the beating heart on his chest. Stun him and use axe finishers to win.

 Both

Defeat Plant 43's 9 buds for the achievement. Parry tentacles and shoot the open mouth. Enter the Ark facility for the final act.

 Both

Defeat the Elite Guard Commander in an axe duel (he drops the Death Edge axe). Face Victor in a 3-phase final boss fight with rocket launcher.

Boss Guide

Detailed strategies for every boss fight in RE9

View full boss guide with screenshots →
The Shadow Girl Stealth

Afraid of light. Always keep room lights on. When she blocks your path to Room 203, detour to Room 201's light. Use empty bottles to distract her (but save them for crafting). Crouch through holes to bypass her. Final fight: activate 3 power systems, use searchlights to stun her, then open the roof for sunlight.

Chef Zombie Easy

First encounter: pure stealth. Hide when he says "Next dish" and stay far when he says "No, no." Push the cart while he's chopping. Second encounter (outside recreation room): use Requiem on his head first, then finish with the pistol. Drops the Food Storage Key.

Fat Zombie (Sanatorium) Medium

Lure him back to the Research Director's room — he gets stuck in the doorway. Use Requiem on his head, then pistol shots. If he leaves, lure him back and repeat. Drops the Watcher Charm. As Leon on 3F: grab the MSBG 500 Shotgun first, shotgun to head + axe finisher. He can break walls revealing hidden supplies.

Spore Zombie Hard

Leon's sniper section in the church. Shoot the glowing body parts. Keep moving in circles to avoid attacks. Use gas canisters for massive damage. When Spore Heads spawn, use grenades to clear them. When stunned, rush in for physical attacks. Pick up dropped weapons to throw at him.

Titan Spider Hard

Initially can only flee. Cut webs with the axe. Lure it to gas canisters to flip it over, then attack the glowing weak point on its abdomen with the shotgun. It sprays poison from behind — that's also an attack window. There are two gas canisters to use, plus shotgun ammo scattered around.

Tyrant Hard

Chase phase: just run. Don't waste ammo. Navigate through the police station to safety. Boss fight: parry his melee attacks with the axe (Spacebar / L1 / LB). Aim for the beating heart on his chest — wait for crosshair for bonus damage. Stun him and use axe finishers. When he stands on the car, unload everything at his chest.

Plant 43 Medium

Shoot all 9 flower buds for the "Not Today, Little Buds!" achievement. Parry tentacles with the axe, then shoot the open flower buds. For the main body: dodge the two side tentacles, wait for the center mouth to open, then blast it with your strongest weapon. Shoot the fuel tank to burn it for the kill.

Elite Guard Commander Extreme

Appears similar to HUNK. Bullets barely hit him — pure axe combat. Parry his attacks and counter with axe swings. Sometimes parries can stun him for physical attacks. SMG works at range if you catch him in animations, but switch to axe when he closes in. Drops the Death Edge axe upon defeat.

Victor (Final Boss) Extreme

Phase 1: Parry tentacle attacks with axe, shoot his head between parries. Phase 2: He charges on high ground — unload into his head. Phase 3: He pulls out a rocket launcher — shoot his head twice with Requiem to make him drop it, pick it up and fire. Final phase: shoot glowing pustules on head and both tentacle arms. Repeat until all pustules explode.

Puzzle Solutions

All puzzle answers in one place — spoilers ahead

View full puzzle guide with screenshots →
Chairman's Room Puzzle Box
Moon → Sun → Star → Moon

Open the Unicorn music box for the Ruby. Find the pencil on the Chairman's desk. Use it to shade the missing page notebook (in the trash can: "Note to the Chairman") to reveal the answer. Rewards: Moon Crystal.

Research Director's Puzzle Box
Star → Sun → Moon → Sun

Use Metal Dissolving Fluid to open the puzzle box. Combine clues from Director's Photos 1 & 2 (Photo 3 is for Chairman's room). Rewards: Sun Crystal + Leather-bound Notebook (Spencer's Research Report).

Blood Research Lab (Laser Microscope)
Activate all atoms in one step

Use the laser microscope to analyze the purple blood sample. One-step activation unlocks Pistol Ammo and Hemolytic Syringe recipes. Second analysis unlocks Steroid and Stabilizer recipes. Third unlocks Medical Syringe and 12.7x55mm Requiem Ammo recipes.

Organ Transport Box
Dial → HAND / Switch → Middle / Press 5 / Press 2 / Dial → AUTO

Found in the garage. Examine in inventory, open side cover. Follow the "Organ Transport Box Unlock Method" file instructions in exact order. Rewards: Artificial Lung.

VIP Room Wine Bottle
Rotate bottle to align grape pattern, then extend

Find the wine bottle on the table next to the weapon rack. Rotate it so the grape patterns match up, then extend the bottle. This produces the Double Helix Model. Place it on the central stand to reveal the VIP room is actually an elevator.

S.T.A.R.S. Office Gun Cabinet
R R R

Check Barry's desk for his to-do list. Wesker borrowed a book. Wait by Wesker's desk for the Library Card to appear. Find the book in the library upper floor — inside is Rebecca's photo. The three words on the back start with R. Enter RRR for the gun cabinet.

Locker Key (Treasure Hunt)
Climb crates in the Command Room

Find Clue 1 at Chris's coat and Clue 2 in the S.T.A.R.S. office cabinet. Follow both clues to the Command Room — climb the wooden crates to reach the Locker Key at the top. Opens the locker in the West Office for the S.T.A.R.S. Badge Charm.

Room 201 Fuse Box
Use screwdriver to open fuse box, take fuse

Move the push cart to the bookshelf in the Nurse's Station area. Climb up to reach the box containing the Screwdriver. Go to Room 201, use the screwdriver on the fuse box to get the Fuse. Use it on the shutter door to trigger the next story sequence.

Crafting Recipes

All unlockable crafting recipes and where to find them

💥

Pistol Ammo + Hemolytic Syringe

Blood Research Lab. Use the Blood Collector to gather infected blood from marked locations. Analyze the purple blood sample in the laser microscope (activate all atoms in one step).

💪

Steroid + Stabilizer

Requires ID Bracelet (Level 1). Find the blood sample in the small room next to the Blood Research Lab. Analyze in the laser microscope to unlock. Need upgraded Blood Collector capacity to craft.

💊

Medical Syringe + Requiem Ammo (12.7x55mm)

Find the blood sample in the storage room next to the Waiting Room. Analyze at the Blood Research Lab laser microscope. Requiem ammo is critical — save it for boss fights.

🔥

Molotov Cocktail

Sanatorium basement, in the rest area supply box (after Leon's story section begins). Craft from empty bottles — highly effective against groups and the Shadow Girl.

⚗

Sulfuric Acid Bottle

ARK map, Guard Room (next to the safe). Craft from empty bottles. Essential for fighting Lickers. Also unlocks a bonus recipe: 5 Antique Coins = 1 Scrap.

💫

Key Materials

Empty Bottles: found throughout the game, used for crafting Molotovs and Sulfuric Acid. Infected Blood: marked on map after getting Blood Collector. Save bottles — they're valuable for crafting.

Difficulty Modes

Choose the right challenge for your playstyle

🎯

Casual

For first-time players. Extremely generous resources — ammo and healing items everywhere. Enemies go down with fewer shots. Very forgiving; you won't get stuck due to lack of supplies. Can switch to this from Standard Modern at the game over screen (but not back).

⚖

Standard — Modern

The balanced default. Best for players familiar with modern Resident Evil games (RE7/RE8/RE2 Remake). Moderate resources, enemies deal fair damage. You'll need to think about resource management but won't be overwhelmed. Can switch down to Casual at the game over screen.

📜

Standard — Classic

For series veterans and classic RE fans. This is the actual high-difficulty mode. Fewer auto-save points. Grace's route requires Ink Ribbons to manually save. Tighter resource management demands careful planning. The authentic survival horror experience.

☠

Madness (Insanity)

Unlocked after beating any difficulty. Recommended to start from New Game+ so you can carry over weapons and upgrades. Extreme enemy damage, scarce resources. Required for 100% completion. Start with NG+ to inherit your previous loadout.

All Endings

3 endings — how to unlock each one (spoilers)

Bad Ending
Chapter of Destruction
Trigger: Choose to destroy Elpis (no cure)

Leon sacrifices himself to cover Grace's retreat, fighting the Wesker clone Zeno to the death. Leon is executed point-blank. Grace escapes the lab alone. Zeno falls into the abyss as the platform collapses. Non-canon, tragedy-only branch.

Good Ending (Canon)
Rebirth of Hope
Trigger: Choose to release Elpis (global cure)

Grace injects the concentrated vaccine into Leon, purging the virus and rejuvenating him. Victor kills Zeno and injects a parasite, mutating into a Pursuer form — Leon defeats him. Chris arrives with BSAA. Leon and Grace are rescued. The global virus is cleansed. Post-credits: Sherry is also cured, Umbrella's legacy is finally settled.

Hidden Ending

Trigger: Abandon the researcher in Sanatorium + collect 3 faded diaries + 5 infected blood samples + 3 night explorations

Grace's route exclusive. Uncovers the truth that her mother was silenced by Umbrella. She drives a fuel tanker into the virus research institute, destroying all evidence. Unlocks the Alyssa Ashcroft Memorial Album. Post-credits code: wrenwood_1998 unlocks a Raccoon City Hotel mini-game.

Essential Tips

12 crucial tips for survival

View full tips guide with screenshots →
1

Quick Turn Saves Lives

Keyboard: X / Controller: Left stick back + Circle (PlayStation) or Left stick back + B (Xbox). Use this to instantly 180-degree turn when danger appears behind you.

2

Leon's Axe Counter

When Leon is grabbed by a zombie, follow the prompt to counter with the axe. The axe can also be used for stealth kills and finisher attacks on stunned enemies.

3

Physical Attacks on Stunned Enemies

When a zombie is stunned, look for the prompt to use a physical attack. These deal significant damage and can save ammo.

4

Throw Dropped Weapons at Groups

Zombies sometimes drop weapons on death. If other zombies are nearby, follow the prompt to throw those weapons at them — free damage.

5

Always Check Car Trunks

Many vehicles throughout the game have supplies in their trunks. Make it a habit to check every car you pass for ammo and healing items.

6

Keep Room Lights ON

When you wake up in Room 203 and visit Room 201, turn on the lights and leave them on. This is critical for surviving the Shadow Girl encounter — she fears light.

7

Push Carts Alert Monsters

Some high-up items require push carts to reach. Be careful — dragging push carts makes noise that attracts monsters. Clear the area first or have an escape route ready.

8

Save Empty Bottles

Empty bottles can distract monsters when thrown, but they're also crafting materials for Molotov Cocktails and Sulfuric Acid Bottles. Don't waste them on distractions unless necessary.

9

Watch Out for Active Chainsaws

Chainsaws dropped by dead zombies keep spinning on the ground. Walking near them causes damage. Wait for them to stop, then pick them up. But kill nearby zombies first — they can grab chainsaws too.

10

Grace's Physical Attack is Push Only

Unlike Leon, Grace can only push zombies down with physical attacks. Use the push to create distance, then shoot the downed zombie in the head.

11

Hemolytic Syringe = Permanent Kill

Use the Hemolytic Syringe for stealth kills from behind, or to permanently finish stunned/play-dead zombies. Without it, downed zombies will eventually revive. Always carry a few.

12

Doorway Cheese for Fat Zombies

Fat Zombies are too wide for standard doorways. Lure them to a door and they get stuck. Use this safe spot to shoot their head with Requiem + pistol. Works in both the Research Director's room and the 3F attic.
