Boss Guide

Strategies for every boss fight in RE9

The Chef Easy

Optional boss. Weak to shotgun blasts and fire grenades. Circle around the kitchen island to avoid grabs. Drop an explosive barrel when he charges.

The Girl (Shadow) Medium

Recurring puzzle-boss. Cannot be damaged conventionally. Use UV lamps to expose her, then interact with the environment. Pattern changes each encounter.

Plant 43 Medium

Shoot the exposed orange cores on its tendrils. Keep distance from the acid spray. Magnum or rifle works best. Use herbicide canisters in the room.

Chunk Hard

Heavy brute with charge attacks. Shoot the glowing weak point on his back after he charges past you. Shotgun staggers work well. Bring plenty of first aid.

Titan Spinner Hard

Large mutated creature with spinning limb attacks. Use rifle for precise weak-point shots. Dodge the AOE slam by staying at mid-range. Explosive rounds are highly effective.

Mr. X Hard

Unstoppable stalker during Chapter 7. Cannot be killed — only staggered temporarily. Use flash grenades to buy 5 seconds. Learn the map routes and safe rooms.

The Commander / Hunk Extreme

Fast, aggressive human boss with a machine gun and flashbangs. Use cover, parry his melee combos, and counter-attack during reload windows. Magnum to the head.

Super Tyrant Extreme

Three-phase final boss. Phase 1: dodge charges, shoot chest. Phase 2: use rocket launcher from supply drop. Phase 3: timed QTE sequence. Save your best ammo for this.

Victor Gideon Extreme

Main antagonist and penultimate boss. Mutates mid-fight. Exploit his elemental weakness — fire during mutation, ice rounds for his armored state. Bring a full loadout.

Difficulty Modes

New naming system — choose your challenge

🎯

Casual

Strong aim assist, generous health, weak enemies. Perfect for story-focused players. Equivalent to RE7/RE8 "Assisted" mode.

⚖

Standard — Modern

Balanced default experience. Free saving, normal enemy health/damage. The recommended way to play your first run.

📜

Standard — Classic

Same base difficulty as Modern but requires Ink Ribbons to save. Tighter resource management for the authentic RE experience.

☠

Insanity

The ultimate challenge. Replaces Nightmare/Madhouse. Extreme enemy damage, scarce resources, and no mercy. Unlock after beating the game once.

Crafting Guide

Key recipes and materials you need to know

💥

Small Gunpowder

Craft handgun and SMG ammo. Most common material — found in desk drawers, lockers, and enemy drops.

💣

Large Gunpowder

Craft shotgun shells, rifle rounds, and grenades. Rarer than small gunpowder. Prioritize shotgun ammo.

⚖

Rare Metal

Grace-exclusive upgrade material. Used to enhance the Kotetsu and Freya's Needle. Found in hidden areas.

💊

First Aid Spray

Combine Green Herb + Green Herb for standard heal. Add Red Herb for full heal. Craft at any workbench.

🔥

Incendiary Grenade

Large Gunpowder + Chemical. Deals fire DoT in an area. Extremely effective against Plant 43 and Blister Heads.

💫

12.7x55 Ammo

Special ammo for the "Requiem" weapon. Cannot be crafted — only found in limited quantities. Save for the final boss.

Essential Tips

Get the most out of your first playthrough

1

Aim for the Knees

Shooting zombie knees drops them instantly, buying you time without wasting headshot ammo. Follow up with a stomp or knife finisher.

2

Switch Perspectives Freely

Use first-person for tight corridors and immersive horror. Switch to third-person for combat-heavy sections and better spatial awareness.

3

Learn the Parry Timing

Leon can parry melee attacks with the hatchet. The window is generous — press block just as the attack lands. Counter-attacks deal massive damage.

4

Don't Kill Everything

As Grace, avoiding combat saves precious ammo. Learn patrol routes and use distractions (throw bottles, set off car alarms).

5

Focus on Crit Upgrades First

Handgun crit upgrades give the best ROI. A crit headshot deals 3x damage — potentially one-shotting regular zombies.

6

Save Magnum Ammo for Bosses

Magnum rounds are extremely scarce. Never use them on regular enemies. Each round should count against a boss weak point.

7

Disarm Weapon Zombies

Zombies carrying weapons drop them on death. You can pick up and use their weapons — a free ammo source in early chapters.

8

Explore Every Room

RE9 rewards exploration with hidden weapons, CP tokens, and lore files. Check behind furniture and inside locked drawers (need lockpicks).
