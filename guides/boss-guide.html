RE9 Boss Guide — All Boss Strategies

Source: entertainment14.net / Ali213

The Shadow Girl Monster

Don’t panic — this monster is afraid of light. Quickly run to Room 203 (make sure the lights are ON) and you’ll be safe.

After the monster leaves, go to the nurse’s station and pick up a Medical Syringe.

Move the body aside — the drawer is empty. The storage cabinet blocked by the cart contains a Green Herb.

To reach the box up high you need to use the push cart. Be careful though — dragging the cart makes noise that attracts the monster.

When the monster hasn’t spotted you, throwing empty bottles can distract it. However, since empty bottles can later be used to craft Molotov Cocktails and Sulfuric Acid Bottles, try to conserve them.

You don’t actually need to throw bottles here. When the monster approaches, crouch and crawl through the hole to bypass it.

On your way back to Room 203, the monster may appear and block your path. Turn around and head to the light in Room 201.

After the monster leaves, return to the nurse’s station. Move the cart next to the bookshelf, then open the box on the shelf to get the [Screwdriver].

On the way to Room 201, the monster will appear to intercept you. Turn around and hide in Room 203.

After it leaves, exit Room 203. On your way to Room 201 again, the monster will burst through the nurse’s station door to intercept you.

At this point the light in Room 203 goes out. Circle around inside Room 203 to shake off the monster, or circle around the nurse’s station.

Then head to Room 201. Use the screwdriver to open the fuse box and obtain the [Fuse].

After getting the fuse, the monster will chase you again. Quickly install the fuse at the shutter door to trigger a cutscene.

Chef Zombie

First Encounter

Swipe the keycard to enter the west side kitchen and encounter the Chef Zombie.

You don’t need to fight the Chef Zombie right now — just avoid him. You only have one bullet and can’t kill him, so don’t waste it.

Hide and stay undetected. Wait until he’s chopping food, then push the cart while he’s distracted.

Note: when he says "next dish," he’ll turn to look at you. You must crouch behind cover or he’ll spot you.

Also watch out — when he says "no, no," he’ll come to the cart area to grab things. Hide further away.

While he’s chopping, push the cart through, then cross the kitchen and exit through the door.

Outside the Recreation Room

The Chef Zombie will now be wandering outside the Recreation Room. If you use the Ruby to enter directly, he’ll trap you inside.

It’s recommended to bring the Requiem revolver and deal with the Chef Zombie outside first — there’s more space to maneuver.

First, shoot the Chef Zombie in the head with the Requiem, then follow up with several handgun headshots to defeat him and obtain the [Pantry Key].

Fat Zombie

On the way to the Seminar Room, you’ll spot an ID Wristband on a corpse. Just as you’re about to grab it, the Fat Zombie appears.

Don’t go downstairs — it will chase you down there.

Quickly retreat to the Research Director’s Office — the Fat Zombie will get stuck in the doorway. Shoot its head with the Requiem, then use the handgun on its head. If it retreats, lure it back to the doorway and continue shooting. Defeat it to obtain the [Watcher’s Pendant].

Then return to the corridor to pick up the [ID Wristband (Level 1)].

Fat Zombie — Sanatorium 3rd Floor (Leon)

Location: Sanatorium — 3rd Floor

The story switches to Leon. Leave the Medical Supply Storage Room.

You’ll encounter the Fat Zombie feeding. Pick up the [Weapon] MSBG 500 Shotgun next to it, then the fight begins.

Shoot its head with the shotgun. When it collapses to the ground, quickly move in and execute it with the axe.

Lure it toward a doorway and it will get temporarily stuck — seize the opportunity to unleash heavy fire on its head.

Explore the attic area to scavenge supplies and familiarize yourself with the layout.

Note: there are supplies hidden inside walls. Lure the Fat Zombie to break through walls so you can collect them.

Look up — there are ladders you can climb for additional supplies.

Shoot its head from an elevated position, then perform a jumping axe execution.

Defeating the Fat Zombie drops a Shock Grenade.

Spore Zombie

Leon’s sniper section. Pass through the cemetery and enter the church. Shoot the Spore Zombie’s glowing weak points with the shotgun. Keep moving in circles during the fight to avoid its attacks.

After clearing regular enemies, Spore Head Zombies will appear. Throw grenades when multiple Spore Heads cluster together. There are also gas canisters nearby to throw — make sure these high-damage items hit the Spore Zombie directly.

When the Spore Zombie is stunned, rush in and use physical attacks. Weapons dropped by minions can also be thrown. After using up explosives and shotgun ammo, finish it off with a few handgun shots.

Final Showdown — The Shadow Girl

Collection Progress on This Page:

After Leon’s sniper section, you arrive at the Water Treatment Plant. The Material Warehouse has a save point. Set Emily down and pick up the [File] Ceiling Operating Instructions from the desk.

Bring the Requiem — it’s time for the final showdown with the Shadow Girl.

Operate the ceiling opening device — you’ll find the ceiling won’t open.

Take the ladder next to the elevator down to the lower level and activate the three power supply systems.

The Shadow Girl will try to stop you. Don’t fight — just evade. If you can’t dodge, use a Molotov Cocktail or the Requiem to temporarily stop it.

Near the power supply systems in the Storage Area and Chemical Processing Room, there are searchlights. Use the searchlight to temporarily freeze the Shadow Girl, then quickly activate the power system. Once activated, the lights come on and the area becomes safe.

The Operations Control Room’s power system is missing its control lever. Go to the adjacent Archives Room to pick up the [Crank Lever Type 3].

After activating all three power supply systems, take the elevator back to the upper level.

There’s a searchlight near the ceiling device. Use it to temporarily freeze the monster, then operate the ceiling opening device.

Open the ceiling to let sunlight flood in on the monster. Finish it off with one final shot.

Titan Spider

At the NF Building, you can only flee at first. Spider webs can be cut with the axe.

After going upstairs, you’ll encounter many small spiders — toss a grenade to clear them. As you proceed, the Titan Spider will ambush you through a window. Climb the ladder upstairs to begin the real fight.

Don’t attack head-on. First, scavenge supplies from the surrounding area while avoiding it. It’s recommended to run to the right side first, then come back to climb the ladder ahead and loot above.

On the other side of the building, you’ll find a gas canister. Lure the spider near it, then shoot the canister to detonate it.

After the explosion, it will rear up and expose its rear. Unleash shotgun blasts on its weak point (the rear).

After it recovers, climb the nearby ladder — there’s another gas canister above.

Use the gas canister to blast it again, then hammer its rear weak point with the shotgun.

Then circle around it, looking for opportunities to attack its rear. It will frequently turn and spray poison from its rear — this is also an attack opportunity.

Explore the area for shotgun ammo pickups, plus gunpowder you can use to craft more shotgun shells.

Tyrant Chase Sequence

The story switches back to Leon. Don’t waste ammo on the Tyrant — just run.

Pass through the Reporter’s Room and go upstairs. At the Waiting Room, the Tyrant suddenly thrusts an iron bar through — quickly run back, at least to the corridor corner, or you’ll be killed.

Chased to the Chief’s Office, the Tyrant will smash through the wall from the Private Collection Room. Turn back through the corridor to the Private Collection Room, pass through the Waiting Room, and reach the 2nd Floor Lobby to be safe.

After the cutscene, check beside you — at the end of the 2nd Floor Lobby corridor — to pick up [File 65/75] City Brochure.

Final Tyrant Boss Fight

Time your parries carefully — press (Spacebar / L1 / LB) to parry the Tyrant’s melee attacks with your axe. If done correctly, he basically can’t hurt you.

When he stops and faces you, use the Requiem or other powerful weapons to aim at his beating heart on his chest. Wait for the crosshair to focus before shooting for higher damage.

Hitting the heart can stagger his attacks, giving you time to prepare for the next parry. With practice, you’ll be the only one dealing damage.

When he stands still on the car, don’t hesitate — unleash everything on his chest. A burst from the SMG works well too.

After stunning him, move in and execute with the axe. Repeat this cycle a few times to defeat the Tyrant.

After defeating the Tyrant, scavenge the battlefield area. You can return to the save point via the basketball court to the east.

Plant 43 (9 Flower Buds)

After defeating the Tyrant, head north into the Orphanage. Crawl through the hole in the floor.

Emerge from the sewers, cross the log bridge to reach the subway station.

Inside the train, you’ll encounter Plant 43’s buds. Shoot the open flower buds to repel the vines.

Destroy all 9 buds to earn the trophy achievement "Not Today, Little Buds!" This is [Bud 1] — impossible to miss. There are 8 more ahead.

Cross through the train at the platform. Pick up the supply crate at the location shown, then grab the rope and rappel down.

At the bottom of the pit, encounter [Bud 2]. Destroy it and proceed — you’ll see something hanging up high.

Be sure to explore the side paths — there are items to collect.

On the way to the southern fork, encounter two buds [Bud 3][Bud 4] at the junction. There’s also [Bud 5] along the side path.

Continue forward and encounter [Bud 6].

Be careful not to walk near the root stems on the ground or you’ll be grabbed. Shoot them from a distance, then take out [Bud 7].

Continue forward and destroy the last two buds [Bud 8][Bud 9] guarding the path.

Proceed until tentacles block your path. Parry the tentacle attacks with your axe, then shoot the flower buds when they open.

After destroying the tentacles, wait for the left flower bud to open and shoot it — the vines will clear the path.

Finally, fight Plant 43 itself. Tentacles on both sides will alternately lash at you — parry with the axe or sprint to dodge. When the central mouth opens, unleash your most powerful weapons.

When it starts to collapse, shoot the fuel canister to burn it completely. Then open the door to enter The Ark.

Elite Guard Commander

Proceed to encounter the Elite Guard Commander (outfit resembles HUNK). Firearms are largely ineffective against him — you need to fight up close with the axe.

Time your axe parries against his attacks, then counter with axe swings. Repeat this pattern — there are also some spectacular axe-clashing sequences.

Sometimes a successful parry will stun him, allowing you to follow up with a physical attack.

If you insist on using guns, time your SMG bursts well — enough hits will land. But stay focused: once he closes in, switch back to axe parries immediately.

Defeat the Elite Guard Commander to obtain his axe [Weapon] — Lethal Edge. Return upstairs to the Central Refining System to trigger a cutscene.

Victor (Final Boss)

There are plenty of supplies scattered around. Parry Victor’s tentacle attacks with the axe to minimize damage. Deflect all his attacks, then unleash heavy firepower on his head.

After completing Phase 1, he’ll collapse to the ground. Use this window to keep shooting his head.

Phase 2 begins — he’ll stand on an elevated position and charge up. Use this time to unleash maximum firepower on his head.

In the final phase, he’ll pull out a Rocket Launcher. Shoot him in the head twice with the Requiem to make him drop it. Quickly pick up the Rocket Launcher and fire at him to enter Phase 3.

In the final phase, the weak points are obvious — shoot his head and the glowing pustules on the tentacles on both sides.

After destroying all three pustules, he’ll collapse and expose more pustules. Use the shotgun or SMG to quickly destroy them all.

Repeat this process once more. He’ll grab you — break free and the battle ends.
