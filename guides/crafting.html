RE9 Crafting Guide — All Recipes & Locations

Source: entertainment14.net / Ali213 — Translated to English

Handgun Ammo & Hemolytic Syringe Crafting Recipes

Location: Blood Research Lab

Arrive at the Blood Research Lab and pick up the [Blood Collector] on the desk. Collect infected blood from various locations to craft items. All infected blood sources are already marked on the map — don't worry, they won't disappear if you don't collect them right away. You'll gradually use them all up as you explore.

On the desk you'll also find [File 15/75] — Synthesis of Blood Compounds.

Use the Laser Microscope to analyze the purple blood sample next to it. You only need one step to activate all atoms, unlocking the [Handgun Ammo Crafting Recipe] and [Hemolytic Syringe Crafting Recipe].

Steroid & Stabilizer Crafting Recipes

With the ID Wristband (Level 1), you can now open the four Level 1 security doors on the first floor.

In the small compartment next to the Blood Research Lab, pick up [File 25/75] — Victor's Notes, an Empty Syringe, and a Blood Sample.

Use the Laser Microscope in the Blood Research Lab to analyze the blood sample, unlocking the [Steroid Crafting Recipe] and [Stabilizer Crafting Recipe]. The Blood Collector's capacity is currently insufficient, so you can't craft Steroid or Stabilizer with the Empty Syringe just yet.

Medical Syringe & Requiem Ammo (12.7x55mm) Crafting Recipes

In the storage room next to the waiting room, pick up a Blood Sample.

Go to the Blood Research Lab and use the Laser Microscope to analyze the blood sample, unlocking the [Medical Syringe Crafting Recipe] and [Requiem Ammo (12.7x55mm) Crafting Recipe].

Molotov Cocktail Crafting Recipe

Location: Sanatorium — Basement

After Leon's story segment ends, you arrive in the Sanatorium basement.

Use the connector plug to restore power, then head to the lounge. In a box on the desk you'll find the [Molotov Cocktail Crafting Recipe]. There's a Mr. Raccoon nearby.

Sulfuric Acid Bottle Crafting Recipe

From the Ark to the experiment area entrance, use the magnetic key to open the door into the hall. The dual-slot security console requires two orbs.

In the guard room next to the hall, find the [Sulfuric Acid Bottle Crafting Recipe] next to the safe. Craft Sulfuric Acid Bottles from empty bottles to use against Lickers.

This also unlocks a crafting recipe: five Antique Coins can be combined into one Scrap — so collecting all those coins isn't actually very useful.
