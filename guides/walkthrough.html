Full Walkthrough — All Parts

Complete illustrated walkthrough with all collectibles (75 files, 25 Mr. Raccoons, 20 antique coins)

Click any part below to read the full illustrated walkthrough. Each part contains detailed step-by-step instructions with screenshots for every section of the game.

Part 1

Sanatorium — Grace's Escape

Wake up in Room 203. Navigate the shadow girl, find the screwdriver, solve the Chairman's puzzle, explore the Blood Research Lab, and survive the Chef Zombie.
Part 2

Sanatorium Deep — Leon's Combat Section

Switch to Leon. Fight the Fat Zombie on 3F, pick up the MSBG 500 Shotgun. Explore the attic, underground, and basement areas.
Part 3

Cemetery & Water Treatment Plant

Leon's sniper section. Defeat the Spore Zombie in the church. Reach the water treatment plant. Final showdown with the Shadow Girl using searchlights.
Part 4

NF Building & Police Station

Fight the Titan Spider. Visit the Raccoon City Police Station. Explore S.T.A.R.S. office with nostalgic callbacks. Tyrant chase sequence.
Part 5

Tyrant Boss & Plant 43

Defeat the Tyrant in a parry-heavy boss fight. Navigate the sewers. Fight Plant 43 and its 9 flower buds. Enter the Ark facility.
Part 6

The Ark — Final Chapters

Explore the Ark facility. Defeat the Elite Guard Commander. Face Victor in the 3-phase final boss fight. Choose your ending.
