Examination Room Safe

Examination Room Safe Code: Right 30, Left 10, Right 50 (Madness: Left 50, Right 30, Left 90)

These codes are provided for quick reference. For the intended experience, we recommend finding the clues in-game before opening safes.

Swipe the keycard to enter the East Reception Room. On the counter you’ll find [Mr. Raccoon 2/25].

Head to the Examination Room and pick up the Hemolytic Syringe.

Use the Hemolytic Syringe to stealth-kill zombies from behind, or finish off stunned zombies. Zombies playing dead will eventually revive — use the Hemolytic Syringe to permanently kill them.

Ignore the Examination Room safe for now — you’ll find the code elsewhere. If you insist on opening it now: Right 30, Left 10, Right 50.

How to Find the Code

Bring plenty of ammo and the Requiem revolver.

Take the stairs east of the Waiting Room upstairs. Pick up the [East Wing Map] from the wall, then enter the Research Director’s Office for [Lockpicks] and a save point.

In the inner office of the Research Director’s Room, pick up the puzzle-related files: [File 17/75] Director’s Message, [File 18/75] Director’s Photo 1, [File 19/75] Director’s Photo 2, [File 20/75] Director’s Photo 3.

On the way to the Seminar Room, you’ll spot an ID Wristband on a corpse. Just as you’re about to grab it, the Fat Zombie appears.

Don’t go downstairs — it will chase you down there.

Quickly retreat to the Research Director’s Office — the Fat Zombie will get stuck in the doorway. Shoot its head with the Requiem, then use the handgun. If it retreats, lure it back and continue. Defeat it to obtain the [Watcher’s Pendant].

Then return to the corridor to pick up the [ID Wristband (Level 1)].

Pick up [File 21/75] Examination Room Safe Code at the location shown.

Sanatorium Basement Safe

Standard (Modern) Difficulty: Right 60, Left 40, Right 80

Madness Difficulty: Left 70, Right 50, Left 20

How to Find the Code

After Leon defeats the Fat Zombie in the 3rd floor attic, pry open the cabinet on the west side to obtain the Alligator handgun compensator and the basement safe code.

S.T.A.R.S. Office Armory Lockbox

S.T.A.R.S. Office Armory Lockbox Code: RRR

How to Find the Code

At the Raccoon City Police Station, climb the ladder from the reception area upstairs to the Lounge. In the S.T.A.R.S. Office you’ll find nostalgic callbacks: Rebecca’s medical bag, Jill’s beret, and Chris’s jacket.

On Barry’s desk, pick up [File 59/75] Barry’s To-Do List. You learn that Captain Wesker borrowed a book from the Library and never returned it.

Wesker’s office is a separate partition. You can see the old group photo of the S.T.A.R.S. team.

Open Wesker’s desk drawer — it’s empty at first. Wait a moment and [File 60/75] Library Card will appear. You learn the borrowed book is "The Medicinal Value of Herbs."

Take the stairs to the Library upper floor. Find Wesker’s borrowed book and open it to discover Rebecca’s photo inside. Wesker, how many photos of Rebecca did you hide?!

The first letters of the three words on the back of Rebecca’s photo form the code: RRR. Obtain [File 61/75] Note on Back of Photo.
