RE9 Walkthrough Part 2 — Sanatorium Deep: Leon's Combat Section

Source: entertainment14.net / Ali213

Sanatorium Deep — Blood Research & Exploration

Next, go to the body on the left and pick up the Herb and the severed limb.

We can find an "East Wing Keycard" on the severed limb.

After getting the keycard, let's head to the East Wing.

Walk over and swipe the card.

After entering, destroy the "Mr. Raccoon" figurine on the desk.

After picking it up, go further inside. In the room ahead, there's a body missing organs. We can only observe it for now — it doesn't have a practical use yet.

In the cabinet on the right, there's a safe. We don't have the code yet, so skip it for now.

You can see a zombie in the next room. Before fighting it, pick up the "Hemolytic Syringe" item on the right side of the desk.

This item is an incredibly useful tool for dealing with zombies.

It can instantly kill a zombie from behind, or be used when a zombie is staggered (essentially a stealth kill).

After injecting, the zombie will burst open.

Then go through the door. In the hallway, go straight and enter the next door.

Found "Scrap" in the cabinet.

On the desk, you'll find the key item "Blood Collection Device."

The Blood Collection Device can be used to craft items.

Some crafting recipes require analyzing blood samples with the Laser Microscope. I'll show you how below.

We can now hold F at these blood-filled barrels to collect blood with the Blood Collection Device. On the desk, there's a "Blood Compound Synthesis" book.

Next to the computer, you can get a "Blood Sample (Mutation)."

After getting this sample, interact with the machine on the left to start blood analysis.

Select "Blood Sample (Mutation)" on the right to begin the analysis.

You'll enter an operation interface where you need to light up all the atoms. I'm not sure if achieving the "minimum steps" shown in the image gives any bonus, but let's follow those steps anyway.

The first time is simple — just click this one atom.

After activating everything, we can craft Pistol Ammo and Hemolytic Syringes.

After exiting, press TAB to open the crafting menu.

All required crafting materials will be displayed.

With our current materials, we can only choose between crafting Ammo or a Hemolytic Syringe. I recommend the syringe.

The map now shows locations where we can collect blood.

Now let's go back to the hallway and head through this glowing red area.

There will be quite a few zombies in the hallway ahead. If you can't fight them all, just kite and run past.

Keep going forward. There's another Mr. Raccoon on the desk ahead.

Get an Antique Coin from the desk.

At the top of the stairs, there's a map on the wall.

First, enter the door on the left. Inside, there's a Lockpick on the left.

Then go to the next room — it's full of things to interact with.

On the desk behind you, there's a note: "Research Director's Message."

The machine on the right is locked. We can't unlock it yet.

Investigate everything in the room. Pick up the Herb from the cabinet and then head out.

You can save here.

After leaving, go left. There's a body on the ground — pick up the "ID Wristband (Level 1)" from the floor.

We can finally open Level 1 security doors.

However, a Fat Zombie will suddenly appear right in front of us.

Retreat to the room we just came from — it can't fit through the door.

It will return to its original position to block the path. Wait for it to move away, then go down the stairs.

There's actually another door on the left side of the downstairs hallway. Let's go through it.

After opening the door, there's an Antique Coin on the desk.

There are zombies inside too. If they haven't noticed us, ignore them and open the door on the left.

Inside the door, we get the second "Blood Sample (Convergence)."

Inside, there are also crafting materials and Requiem Ammo.

If the zombies from outside followed us in, deal with them after getting your supplies.

Some zombies will directly drop blood bags, saving us the trouble of collecting blood.

Let's head to the lobby and look around — no zombies bothering us now. Here, you can get a "Safe Replacement Notice."

In the opened safe on the right, there are two Antique Coins.

In the drawer behind, there's Scrap.

After collecting everything, exit through the door on the other side.

This large door is actually where we came from before. To the left of the door, there's a drawer that can be lockpicked — the lockpick we got earlier comes in handy. Inside is a rare metal.

We can now go to the Level 1 security door we couldn't access before.

If we take the same route back, new zombies will have spawned here.

The Fat Zombie might also block our path (it basically patrols back and forth in this cross-shaped hallway).

If it blocks the way, circle around and pass by on one side.

Come over and open the Level 1 security door inside the Blood Research Lab.

Inside, there's a lot of stuff again.

But the main thing is to pick up the "Blood Sample (Reversible)."

And nearby, "Victor's Journal."

On the right, there's also an Empty Syringe.

In the corner on the right, there's a Herb.

Then come out and interact with the analyzer on the desk.

First, select the white one.

Click the atoms in the numerical order shown in the image. Four clicks will satisfy the condition.

Exit and open the green one.

Just two clicks to complete it.

Newly unlocked items can be viewed by pressing TAB.

 

After analyzing the blood samples above, let's head back to the West Wing. Craft a Hemolytic Syringe to take out the Chef Zombie. You can save at the lobby first.

The Chef Zombie is most likely wandering around this hallway now.

If you didn't craft a Hemolytic Syringe, don't worry — just pull out "Requiem" and about two shots will take him down. After killing him, you can collect 100 units of blood, and he drops a "Pantry Key."

Now let's head to the kitchen and open the door we couldn't before.

There are many items inside. Scavenge everything.

This "Duty Takeout Charm" boosts knife damage and durability. Just keep it in your inventory for the effect.

After collecting items, go next door. That door is also a Level 1 security door.

There should be a zombie at the door. Deal with it and enter — there's a Herb on the cabinet right behind the door.

After getting the Herb, go left. On the right wall, there's a "Construction Notice."

There's another door on the left. On the cabinet, there's Scrap.

There are Lockpicks in the cabinet.

On the desk, there's a Save point and a "Hex Wrench." If your inventory is full, store items in the Item Box on the right first.

After saving, go out and turn left, then enter the door ahead.

Behind the door is a garage. First, go all the way inside and pick up the Ammo in the corner.

Behind the car, there's an "Organ Transport Case." Interact with it to pick it up.

After picking up the "Organ Transport Case," a zombie will crash in driving a forklift on our way back. We can shoot it (not sure if you die if you don't — haven't tested).

After the forklift stops, we can crawl underneath it.

Go right and break the crate to get Ammo.

If your inventory is full, store items in the Item Box. The "Hex Wrench" must be kept on your person.

After storing items and saving, go out. There's another door across from us — take the stairs up.

After going up, go straight through the large door to reach the office.

On the desk right in front of us, there's a file: "Bar Lounge Safe Code." This is the code for the safe in the bar we saw earlier.

On the desk ahead, there's another Lockpick.

On the nearby desk, there's a "Background Investigation Record." Break the crate for Ammo.

There will be a zombie ambush in the adjacent room.

There's a Simple Knife in this cabinet.

In this cardboard box, there's a "Cleanup Crew Report."

There's Ammo in the crate on the left.

There's an Empty Syringe on the shelf.

After picking it up, go out and turn right. There's a Mr. Raccoon on the sink — destroy it. There's a Herb on the left.

Through this door, we're back at the Bar Lounge we visited earlier. If you didn't open the safe before, you can open it now — we just got the code. The safe opening method was described earlier.

Inside the safe, there are three Antique Coins.

After opening the safe, go down the stairs on this side.

Downstairs is where we broke the wrench trying to open the door earlier. Now that we have the Hex Wrench, we can open it.

Interact with it and select the Hex Wrench.

Hanging above is an "Artificial Heart."

On the right, there's Scrap. In the left corner, there's Ammo.

There's also a blood bag here.

When I was leaving, a zombie I killed earlier had revived. As mentioned before, zombies in this game that aren't killed with headshots may become "Cauliflower" zombies. However, their detection range isn't very far — even though I didn't attack it, it didn't notice me.

Let's go open the Level 1 security door in the nearby Archives room.

Inside, there's Ammo and a Fanny Pack!! Finally, more inventory space!!

By now we should have enough Antique Coins for a second exchange. Let's go to the Recreation Room — the Blood Collection Device capacity upgrade is the most practical.

We've mostly cleared this floor now. Let's go to where the Fat Zombie blocked us before. We can now kill it with three Hemolytic Syringes or three Requiem shots. It's most likely patrolling the cross-shaped hallway.

When passing the Examination Room, we can insert the heart into the body to save an inventory slot.

Killing the Fat Zombie drops a "Watcher Charm."

Keep going forward — the stairs we need to go up are ahead.

After going up the stairs, turn right. This is where the Fat Zombie blocked us before.

First, follow the blood trail on the floor.

Break the crate on the left for a Herb.

There's Scrap in the cabinet on the right.

On the fallen cabinet on the right, there's the "Examination Room Safe Code" — for the room where we inserted the heart.

Turn around and enter the door on the right side.

On the desk on the right side of the corridor, there's an Antique Coin.

Inside, there's another singing zombie. This one has much better detection than the white-clothed one from before. Even a little noise will cause it to sing, making other zombies attack us.

There's Ammo at the entrance. Killing it is relatively easy.

After killing it, you also get an Antique Coin.

On the desk inside, there's a "Patient Record."

On the desk on the left, you'll find the key item "Metal Dissolving Fluid."

After getting this item, go back to the hallway and head right.

We've been to this room before, but without the dissolving fluid, entering won't trigger a cutscene.

Entering with the dissolving fluid triggers a cutscene with Victor, where he reveals some information.

After the cutscene, he forces us outside. Wait for him to leave, then go back into the room.

Enter the room and interact with the padlock. Use the dissolving fluid to melt it open.

Here we need to solve another puzzle by inputting the correct pattern.

The answer is in the photos behind us.

Combine the two photos to figure out the answer.

Select the patterns in this order: "Star, Sun, Moon, Sun" to open the box.

Inside, you get the "Sun Crystal."

There's also a "Leather-bound Notebook."

To the left of the notebook, you can find "Organ Transport Case Unlock Method."

On the right, there's also "Spencer's Research Report Part 1."

Now let's head back to the lobby. Insert the "Sun Crystal" into the main door, and pick up the Organ Transport Case while we're at it. On the way back, stop by the Examination Room to unlock the safe.

Use the code we just obtained.

Inside are three more Antique Coins.

Insert the second crystal at the lobby door.

When saving, you can store the notebook in the Item Box — it doesn't affect our ability to read it.

We now know how to open the Organ Transport Case. We can do it directly from our inventory.

Step 1: Rotate the AUTO button to HAND.

Step 2: Pull the right lever to the middle notch.

Step 3: Pull down the number 5.

Step 4: Pull down the number 2.

Final step: Rotate the HAND button back to AUTO.

After opening the case, you receive the "Artificial Lung."

With the Artificial Lung, we need to go back to the Examination Room. Place the lung inside.

This "patient" will come back to life. Keep your distance, and when it falls to the ground, kill it.

From the body, you'll get the "ID Wristband (Level 2)."

With Level 2 access, let's open the Level 2 security door — the same place where the cutscene occurred.

Come over here and open the door.

After opening the door, go inside. Break the crate on the right for Ammo.

After picking it up, enter the small room. On this box, there's a "Secondary Mutation" file.

On the desk to the right, there's a "Nurse's Journal."

There's Scrap on this equipment.

Push the door open and investigate the notebook on the desk.

Fold down the upper right corner of the notebook to see the puzzle pattern.

There's Ammo on the blackboard.

Then interact with the puzzle machine nearby. It has braille on it, which we can't read.

After a short cutscene, we receive an "ID Wristband (Level 3)." The cutscene directs us to find the little girl.

When we leave, we can see many new zombies have spawned ahead, blocking our only path.

If you're running low on supplies, search the room on the right first — there are some resources.

Fight your way to the lobby and find the little girl.

After the cutscene, we carry her and can't do anything else. If you want to save, place her on the nearby sofa first.

After saving, head out. You'll encounter "Cauliflower" zombies on the way. Since we're carrying Emily, we can't fight. Just dodge and wait for their attack recovery animation, then rush forward.

After entering the room, Emily will hack the code inside while we kill all the zombies outside.

Wait for Emily to call us, then we can go in.

Emily successfully solved the puzzle and got our item, but before she could hand it over, the revived Abnormal Zombie grabbed her.

We can only follow to the underground area where Emily was taken. After jumping down, Grace's cutscene ends.

After Grace jumps down, the perspective shifts to Leon. We wake up in a strange place with Victor right beside us. We secretly cut our ropes, and Victor flees in panic.

We now need to find Victor's office. Press M to open the map.

There's nothing in this room. Go straight up the stairs. There's Ammo in the crate at the stairway.

Enter the door ahead. There's a Herb on the desk.

On the desk in the next room, there's an "Eating Disorder Patient Record."

After getting it, hold F to push aside the cabinet on the right.

After getting through, search for Ammo in this area — it's hidden in various corners.

After searching, move forward. A Fat Zombie is eating a corpse up ahead. There's a Shotgun on the ground — pick it up.

After getting the Shotgun, it will be attracted to us. Don't hold back — shoot it. After a few shots, it will temporarily go down, and we can follow up with melee attacks using left click.

You'll need to repeat this several times to kill it completely. Circle around it in the hallway. The doorframe will get it stuck, letting you get free hits in.

After a while, it will stop trying to squeeze through the door. Go grab the syringe in the wall crack.

While picking up the syringe, it will be attracted to us again and get stuck in the doorframe. But this time, it will eventually smash through. Watch out for its lunging attack.

After the boards are smashed, we can also climb up the stairs on the right side.

There's quite a bit of Ammo to scavenge up here.

It will try to climb up. Shoot it back down.

After shooting it down, you can follow up with a stylish melee attack.

Come back down and give it a few more shots to finish it off.

After killing it, pick up the Grenade from the ground.

Move forward. There's still plenty of Ammo to find in this area.

After getting the Ammo, come over and open the door.

In the cabinet on the right, you can find a Compensator.

The Compensator can be attached to our weapon, slightly reducing muzzle rise.

The note on the right is the "Underground Safe Code."

After getting it, continue downstairs. There's more Ammo on the desk here.

After getting the Ammo, interact with the red switch on the left.

After the ladder drops down, climb down.

At the bottom of the ladder, we're back in the Chairman's Office we visited as Grace earlier.

Opening the map now, we can see that the previously deformed cabinets can be opened by Leon. Let's go open them (I've marked the important items in the images — skip if you don't want them).

The West Wing ward area has some too.

Let's first open the nearby cabinet in the hallway. Some zombies will likely have revived here, but with plenty of Ammo, Leon can easily dispatch them.

In this cabinet, we get Ammo and a "Dishwasher's Note."

Next, open the cabinet in the Data Room. Inside, there's Ammo and a "Mr. Raccoon."

Now go to the West Wing. In the kitchen cabinet, there's a "Sommelier Charm" that increases firearm damage.

The Archives cabinet only has Ammo and a Medical Syringe.

After scavenging the Archives, go straight up the nearby stairs.

On the stairs to the third floor, a zombie will charge at us with a gas canister. Shoot it to blow it up preemptively.

The doors Grace couldn't open before can now be opened by Leon.

Behind the door are piles of bodies. One body has a message telling us to blow their heads off.

I tried shooting a body on the ground, and not only was the zombie not dead, it revived on the spot. (Maybe the message meant to deal with them preemptively?)

More mutated zombies will pour through the door shortly.

After dealing with them, pick up the Ammo on the desk.

You can also break the nearby vase for Ammo.

The zombie in the right corner is playing dead too. Kill it preemptively.

In the next room, you can find a Grenade and a Syringe on the desk.

The zombie on the floor to the left is playing dead.

The mechanism in this room is on the wall. Interact with the painting.

When the door opens, all the zombies behind it will revive.

After dealing with all of them, go through the door behind and take the elevator.

After coming down, a cutscene plays. Leon finds photos of Grace on the computer — unsure what connection she has to all this.

After fighting through as Leon, we return to Grace's perspective.

Walk forward a few steps. On the ground, there's a note: "Staff Member's Last Letter."

Keep going forward. There's an iron door ahead, but we can't open it yet. We can only go left.
