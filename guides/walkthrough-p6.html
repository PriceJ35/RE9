RE9 Walkthrough Part 6 — The Ark: Final Chapters

Source: entertainment14.net / Ali213

Orphanage Ruins & Sewers

A small Easter egg voice message can be found here.

Now head toward the path behind the Tyrant's corpse.

The building ahead is the orphanage, but it has been mostly destroyed. Head down.

After going down you will reach the sewers. Just keep going forward and turn the valve wheel.

After entering, head right and climb out.

Keep walking forward and you will pass through another subway car. When you come out, there is a herb by the cliff edge.

After picking it up, enter the train car on the left. There is ammo on the left side of the car.

You can squeeze through the gap between the two cars ahead.

A mutated plant will appear in the car ahead. Its weak point is the "pistil" in the center. Shoot it a few times and it will retreat.

Your car will fall. You are forced to dismount. Slide down this path.

After sliding down, climb up from the left side.

Find gunpowder on the air conditioning unit.

Then enter the train car. There is scrap on the left.

After picking it up, head right, exit from the right side, and enter the train car opposite.

Enter the opposite car. There is a herb on the right.

Then exit through the front door and loot the supplies in front of you.

After looting, use the zipline on the right.

Hold the S key to slide all the way down to the bottom.

Mutated Plant Gauntlet

Walk forward and crouch through here.

After squeezing through, look up. There is a mutated plant above. You can shoot it to wake it up first.

Wait until it opens its mouth, then attack its weak point. When it sprays a large amount of liquid, it is dead.

Continue forward. The crate on the ground contains ammo.

Walk a few steps forward. There is a path going up here.

There is ammo up top. After grabbing it, go back down.

After coming down, walk forward. There is a mutated plant at the opening ahead. Kill it, then head left.

Walk in a few steps. There is scrap in the pipe on the right.

There are plant "tentacles" on the ground ahead. You can shoot them.

After shooting, its head will appear. Shoot it.

After killing it, continue forward and grab the supplies from the crate.

After grabbing them, go back the way you came to the main path. Before jumping down at this spot, shoot the plant first.

After killing it, there is ammo on the right.

Keep going forward. There are more mutated plants ahead. Shoot the vines below or their heads.

There is ammo on the steps on the right.

After grabbing the ammo, turn around and go straight. There are more well-hidden plants ahead. Shoot them. One on each side.

After killing them, continue forward. There is plenty of ammo on the right.

There are blocking vines ahead. Shoot them to wake them up and expose their weak points.

Two more will suddenly appear ahead.

After repelling them, jump down and grab the ammo on the right.

Giant Mutated Plant Boss

Below is the boss fight. Just like the smaller ones, wait for it to open its mouth and stuff bullets into it.

When its mouth is closed, it will use its left and right vines to swing at you alternately. Just stand in the middle and dodge slightly left or right to avoid them (skilled players can use parry — parry gives invincibility frames).

After repeating this for a few rounds, gas canisters will appear below. Shoot them to blow the boss up and finish it off.

Now go open this large door and enter.

Inside, you can find scrap in the crate on the left.

After grabbing it, walk forward into the green door.

The Ark — Research Facility

After entering the corridor, go left first. The left side has a save point and a room with supplies.

The display screen on the wall can be interacted with to obtain the "N0-AH Status Report".

Upgrade and save, then go back out the same door you came in.

There is a Mr. Raccoon on the right side of the corridor.

After picking it up, go down the stairs all the way. The door ahead can be entered. You will arrive at a room that looks like a server room. Grab the gunpowder on the right.

There is a herb on the cart on the other side.

There is ammo on the desk.

After looting, enter the small door on the other side.

Find 4,500 lei on the desk in the room.

Then interact with the computer next to it. From the surveillance monitors, we discover a group of enemies coming to capture us.

Go back and deal with them in the server room behind you. The enemies have very visible lights on their heads. You can find a spot to crouch and snipe them in the head. The canisters nearby can be shot to release smoke that blocks the enemies' line of sight.

The enemies deal quite a lot of damage, so do not hold back on resources. Dead enemies drop plenty of loot.

After clearing all of them, enter the door on the right.

From this position, you can pick them off one by one with the sniper rifle. Take a shot then return to cover.

After clearing them, grab the ammo on the right and climb the ladder.

After climbing up, stay alert — there are still enemies below.

From up top, shoot this culture pod to release a Licker. After releasing it, jump down — new enemies will spawn.

You can let the Licker deal with the enemies for you.

Lickers in this game are very weak. As long as you do not fire a gun or make loud noises, they will not detect you — even if you are right in their face.

Just watch from nearby. After all the enemies are dealt with, the red door behind will turn green.

Ignore the Licker and head straight inside. The left side of the corridor leads to the save room we visited earlier; the right side is the main path.

Upgrade and save, then take the main path toward the area filled with sculptures.

After coming up, enter the room on the right. There are many supplies to loot. The computer on the wall can be interacted with to read the "Raccoon City Bio-Weapon Research Report".

On the other side, there is "Product Catalog 1" (2026 Edition).

And also "Product Catalog 2" (2026 Edition).

After reading everything, head to the large door in the center.

1v1 Duel — Elite Guard Commander / HUNK

Next up is a 1v1 fight. You must use melee weapons against this enemy. Time to put your parrying skills to the test.

He will dodge all ranged weapons.

Ranged weapons are only effective when he is knocked down or staggered.

Halfway through the fight there is a QTE requiring you to rapidly press F.

He will also break a pipe to release smoke and then move around like a ninja. Chase him and slash, or wait and parry his attacks.

After defeating him, you obtain the "Death Axe".

There are more items to loot in the room.

After looting, go up the stairs, then head left.

After entering the door, a cutscene triggers. Grace and Zeno are here. We get into a confrontation with Zeno and take Grace away with us.

Leon was just beaten by Zeno and his symptoms are becoming more severe. He wants to destroy Elpis but Grace stops him. Grace decides to go stop everything herself and manages to persuade Leon.

Playing as Grace — Escape the Facility

We now control Grace to find a way to escape the abandoned facility.

The crane ahead can be interacted with. Check it out.

Walk over here and Leon will boost you up.

After climbing up, walk forward, open the window in the pipe, and long-press F to jump down.

Climb the stairs and interact with this machine. We do not have the key yet.

The key on the wall is also locked. We now have a new objective: "Find the Override Key".

Keep going up. Pick up the empty bottle by the stairs.

The indoor area on the right is a save point.

Drop anything you do not need into the storage box and save your game.

Head toward this flickering door.

When you reach this glass door, a Licker will walk by.

Pick up the empty bottle on the right.

This Licker will be startled by a nearby noise and come running over.

You can sneak up close and kill it with a syringe.

Pick up the herb on the desk.

There is an empty bottle on the left and a safe on the right. We do not have the combination yet.

There is also a simple knife on the desk on the left.

There is another empty bottle on the other desk.

Now head to the next room. There is also a Licker in the corridor of the neighboring room. You can throw a bottle to lure it over, then kill it with a syringe.

After dealing with both Lickers, you can shoot the zombie lying on the bed in the earlier room to prevent it from ambushing you later.

Then take care of the zombie in the neighboring room.

There is another one hiding in the corner on the left.

After dealing with it, loot the area. You can find a new item on the desk: the "Sulfuric Acid Bottle". This acid is extremely effective against Lickers.

After looting, go pull this key out.

After looting, head back. There will be new zombies in the corridor.

After killing them, enter the door behind them to return to the save point.

Save and then go this way.

A Licker will appear as you go down the stairs. You can ignore it for now and slowly walk down.

Insert the key here to let Leon in.

Pick up the "Magnetic Key" from the wall.

After getting the key, head back up the stairs. The Licker should be on the ground level by now. Use the syringe to kill it.

Use the Magnetic Key to open this red door.

After entering, you can interact with the large door. The game will prompt you to find a way to enter it.

First, enter the door on the left — it is a save point.

Loot the supplies inside. The computer can be interacted with to obtain "Access Log: 51st Meeting Record".

On the left there is a crafting recipe and a safe (we do not have the combination yet). This recipe lets you craft "Sulfuric Acid Bottles".

Craft a few Sulfuric Acid Bottles, then exit and go left.

After going down the stairs, the crate on the right contains a herb and the "Sterilization Room Safe" note.

The "Sterilization Room Safe" is located where we first saw the Lickers. We will go back there later.

Licker Hunting & Sulfuric Acid

There will now be two Lickers in front of us. First, throw an empty bottle at this spot to lure them together.

Then throw a Sulfuric Acid Bottle to burn them both. If you are lucky, the humanoid zombie will come over too. It will not deal much damage, but you may need to finish it off with your gun.

Most of these crates can be opened.

This crate contains ammo.

This crate contains a hip pouch and an empty bottle.

The already-open crate on the other side has scrap.

Walk forward a few steps and new zombies and Lickers will appear. Again, throw a bottle to lure them over and burn them with a Sulfuric Acid Bottle.

Some crates sound like they have zombies inside. Open them and step back a bit. Kill the zombie before going in to grab the supplies.

Behind where the Lickers were, there is another open crate containing scrap and the "Guard Room Safe" combination.

The safe with this code is in the room where we just saved. If you need supplies, go back and open it now. Otherwise, wait until the main path takes you back there.

There is another openable crate in the middle of the path. It also contains a zombie. Kill it, then grab the blood and simple knife inside.

There is also ammo on the right side of the path.

In the middle of the path there is another openable crate with a zombie inside. Kill it, then loot the blood and simple knife.

After looting everything, take the stairs on the other side up.

Noble Sphere & Intellect Sphere

After entering, the computer on the left updates the map. There is a Mr. Raccoon on the right.

Pick up the "Noble Sphere" from the desk on the right. This sphere is used to open a door.

There are other supplies in the room to loot, plus a save point. Save your game.

After saving, climb out from this side.

There is a Licker on the platform. Kill it with a syringe.

Shoot this crate to get some ammo. You can use the sound to lure the next Licker over.

Same technique — kill it with a syringe.

Then loot the supplies. There is another Sulfuric Acid Bottle in this corner.

After looting, jump down from this side.

Go back via this staircase.

After coming up, two more Lickers will rush out. Use the empty bottle + Sulfuric Acid Bottle combo to burn them.

After killing them, walk forward. You can go back to the room on the right to save. If you have not opened the treasure chest yet, you can do so now.

You can now place the sphere you just obtained into this red door.

Then enter this purple door.

After entering, go left and enter the door ahead.

This door leads back to the Sterilization Room. You can come here to open the safe. Enter the combination: Right 10, Left 90, Right 20.

After opening it, you get some supplies.

Then head left and open this red door using the Magnetic Key.

After entering, kill the zombie on the right. There is a Molotov cocktail on the bed.

Pick up the second sphere, the "Intellect Sphere", from the center desk.

After picking up the sphere, many zombies will spawn behind you. Use incendiary ammo to burn them. You will likely need to finish them off with your gun.

After killing them, grab the blood bag on the right and the blood on the ground.

Now go back to the large red door and insert the second sphere.

The large door opens. The computer on the right can be interacted with to obtain "Access Log: 1st Meeting Record".

Then interact with the machine in the center. A cutscene will play. After watching the first half, the view switches back to Leon.

Back to Leon — Pushing Forward

The view returns to Leon. Grace has just cleared the path for us.

After gaining control, climb the ladder up.

Loot the supplies in the room up here.

After looting, head inside. There is a zombie at the corner of the corridor.

Since Leon's body is now at its limit, he will occasionally suffer from negative status debuffs. You need to wait a moment for recovery.

Continue forward. There is a grenade on the right.

This small door is a save point. Loot the supplies, upgrade your gear, save, and then come back out.

After coming out, enter the large door on the right. There are tons of supplies inside. Loot everything.

After looting, exit through the green door on the other side.

Walk all the way down this corridor. About halfway through, Lickers will drop from the ceiling. As Leon, just open fire and go all out.

However, one shot usually will not kill them. You will need to parry the Licker's attack.

After killing the Licker on the platform, there is still one below before going down. You can try to snipe it from above (though it may not die).

There is also a Licker on the opposite platform. At that distance, use the sniper rifle to easily kill it.

If you did not kill the one below, it will be waiting for you in the corner. Be careful.

The crate nearby also has supplies.

Then go interact with this switch.

There is a door ahead you can enter.

After entering, there will be two more Lickers. You can bottleneck them at the door to kill them.

After killing them, loot the supplies inside, then go interact with the switch.

A stairway path has now appeared here.

The zombie in this red pod can be killed early. After killing it, grab the herb from the shelf on the right.

Climb up the stairs and grab the supplies on the desk.

There are also supplies in the corner on the left.

Pick up the "Override Key" from the wall.

After getting the key, exit through the door behind and go left.

Return to the room you were in earlier. This room now has many Lickers spawned on the ceiling.

There are many on the right side too.

But we have gas canisters, so do not worry.

First, take out a grenade and throw it near these Lickers to enter combat mode.

Then pick up a gas canister and throw it at their faces. Shoot it to detonate. This group will all be killed.

The ones on the left may not be caught in the blast, but that is fine — there is another gas canister here.

After blowing them all up, go to the red door and use the key.

Many zombies will emerge from the culture pods at this point. Just open fire with your guns. If you have grenades, throw them.

You can also run around to kite them. They are still quite slow.

After clearing all of them, go in and take the elevator.

Final Chapters & Ending Choices

Get off the elevator and keep dragging your body at its limit forward. The perspective will switch again soon.

The view returns to Grace. She is about to watch another tape.

After the cutscene, we prepare to rescue Leon. Enter through the side door.

The wheelchair in the room can be interacted with.

Go take the elevator nearby.

After exiting the elevator, reunite with Leon. Drag Leon back in front of Elpis. Zeno is still here.

Now you will be given two choices, corresponding to the bad ending and the good ending. It is recommended to see the bad ending first. After the bad ending credits roll, the game will let you choose again whether to see the good ending (if you do not want to see the bad ending, just choose "Extract Elpis" on the right).

Bad Ending: Choosing "Destroy Elpis" leads to the bad ending. No spoilers here — experience it yourself.

Good Ending: Choose "Extract Elpis". For the good ending, you need to fight the final boss, Victor.

Final Boss: Victor — Phase 1

Victor starts with melee attacks, testing your parrying skills (he even uses a rapid multi-hit combo that requires consecutive parries).

After fighting for a while, his main body will use electric attacks. Dodge to the side to avoid them.

When lightning effects appear under your feet, run away early or you will be electrocuted.

Another attack has his main body leaping toward you, causing more and larger lightning effects under your feet. Dodge early.

He also has a charge attack. The author has never successfully parried this one — just sidestep it.

Final Boss: Victor — Phase 2

After the first phase, the second phase begins. The second phase feels easier than the first.

Many red orbs will appear on his body. Shoot them with your gun. The shotgun basically takes one out per shot.

He will occasionally attack with tentacles, which you can still parry (keep an eye on the axe's durability and find a moment to sharpen it).

After clearing the orbs on top, many red orbs will also appear on the bottom. These lower orbs can be shot with the pistol (if you have grenades, you can throw one to destroy them all at once).

Sometimes both left and right tentacles will swing at you simultaneously.

When you see him clutching both arms together, prioritize shooting the red orbs on his hands. Otherwise, he will use a slam attack.

After repeating this for several rounds, he will forcefully grab you. Rapidly press F to escape.

We will deliver the final blow.

After defeating this boss, the rest is pure cutscene. Once the cutscene ends, the game is complete.

Post-Game: Missing Mr. Raccoon Collectibles

The main story is now complete. The author will supplement a few missing Mr. Raccoon collectibles. Four are missing and will be compiled and posted later. One from a New Game+ starting file, and the rest should all be collected (except for Deluxe Edition content).

Right at the start of the game, there is one that can be interacted with on the right side.
