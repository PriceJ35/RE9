RE9 Puzzle Solutions — All Puzzles Guide

Source: entertainment14.net / Ali213

Chairman's Office Puzzle

Pass through the Bar Lounge and enter the Display Room to scavenge.

Examine the white coat to find [File 11/75] Folded Note, which reveals the Chairman has a crystal stone.

Then enter the Chairman's Office across from the Display Room.

Enter the Chairman's Office and turn on the light. Open the Unicorn Ornament Box to obtain the [Ruby].

Ignore the mechanism box puzzle for now — you need to get the pencil from inside first. Pick up [File 12/75] Email to a Manager (Printout) from the nearby desk.

Go further inside to the Chairman's desk. Pick up the [Pencil] from the desk; the drawer has handgun ammo.

Find [File 13/75] Note to the Chairman in the corner trash can.

Go to the sofa area. Use the pencil to shade the missing page in the notebook, obtaining [File 14/75] Chairman's Notes, which reveals the mechanism box answer.

Enter the mechanism box answer: Moon → Sun → Star → Moon to obtain the [Moon Crystal].

You can't use the crystal yet — store it in the item box to save space. Open the east door and go downstairs to return to the save point.

Blood Research Lab Puzzle

Swipe the keycard to enter the East Reception Room. On the counter you'll find [Mr. Raccoon 2/25].

Head to the Examination Room and pick up the Hemolytic Syringe.

Use the Hemolytic Syringe to stealth-kill zombies from behind, or finish off stunned zombies. Zombies playing dead will eventually revive — use the Hemolytic Syringe to permanently kill them.

In the Blood Research Lab, pick up the [Blood Collection Device] from the desk. You can collect infected blood from various locations to craft items. All infected blood locations are already marked on the map — they won't disappear if you leave them for now.

Also on the desk is [File 15/75] Blood Compound Synthesis.

Use the laser microscope to analyze the purple blood sample nearby. Simply activate all atoms in one step to unlock the [Handgun Ammo Crafting Recipe] and [Hemolytic Syringe Crafting Recipe].

Research Director's Office Puzzle

Bring plenty of ammo and the Requiem revolver.

Take the stairs east of the Waiting Room upstairs. Pick up the [East Wing Map] from the wall, then enter the Research Director's Office for [Lockpicks] and a save point.

In the inner office, pick up the puzzle-related files: [File 17/75] Director's Message, [File 18/75] Director's Photo 1, [File 19/75] Director's Photo 2, [File 20/75] Director's Photo 3.

On the way to the Seminar Room, you'll spot an ID Wristband on a corpse. Just as you're about to grab it, the Fat Zombie appears.

Don't go downstairs — it will chase you down there.

Quickly retreat to the Research Director's Office — the Fat Zombie will get stuck in the doorway. Shoot its head with the Requiem, then use the handgun. Defeat it to obtain the [Watcher's Pendant].

Then return to the corridor to pick up the [ID Wristband (Level 1)].

Pick up [File 21/75] Examination Room Safe Code at the location shown.

Save first as there are many zombies ahead. In the Seminar Room, pick up [Antique Coin 12/20]. Defeat the Singer Zombie to get [Antique Coin 13/20].

Inside the Seminar Room, obtain [File 22/75] Patient Record and Metal Dissolving Fluid.

Return to the inner office to encounter Victor. After he leaves, use the Metal Dissolving Fluid to open the mechanism box.

Using Director's Photos 1 and 2 (Photo 3 is for the Chairman's Office), deduce the puzzle answer: Star → Sun → Moon → Sun. Open the mechanism box to obtain the [Sun Crystal] and [Leather-Bound Notebook].

Open the Leather-Bound Notebook to find [File 23/75] Spencer's Research Report Part 1 and [File 24/75] Organ Transport Case Unlock Method.

Go downstairs and use the lockpicks to open the locked drawer in the East Reception Room for Rare Metal.

Go to the Examination Room and enter the code: Right 30, Left 10, Right 50. Open the safe to obtain [Antique Coin 14/20], [Antique Coin 15/20], [Antique Coin 16/20].

Blood Research Lab Puzzle 2

With the ID Wristband (Level 1), you can now open the four Level 1 security doors on the first floor.

In the small room next to the Blood Research Lab, pick up [File 25/75] Victor's Notes, Empty Syringe, and Blood Sample.

Use the laser microscope in the Blood Research Lab to analyze the blood sample. This unlocks the [Steroid Crafting Recipe] and [Stabilizer Crafting Recipe]. The Blood Collection Device capacity is insufficient for now.

In the storage room next to the Waiting Room, pick up a Blood Sample.

Use the laser microscope to analyze the blood sample, unlocking the [Medical Syringe Crafting Recipe] and [Requiem Ammo 12.7x55mm Crafting Recipe].

Answer

Press the buttons in the following order.

Puzzle Walkthrough

The puzzle answer is the reward from the Research Director's Office Puzzle on Page 3 of this guide.

After obtaining the Organ Transport Case in the garage, inspect it in your inventory. Open the side cover and solve the puzzle.

Refer to the file "Organ Transport Case Unlock Method" and follow these steps to open the case and obtain the [Artificial Lung].

1. Turn the knob to HAND.

2. Slide the right switch upward to the middle position.

3. Press button 5.

4. Press button 2.

5. Turn the knob to AUTO.

VIP Room Puzzle

The VIP Room has many interactive items. The wine bottle on the table near the weapon rack is puzzle-related — rotate it to align the grape pattern, then extend the bottle to obtain the [Double Helix Model]. Place the model on the pedestal in the center of the room to reveal that the entire VIP Room is actually an elevator.

Answer

S.T.A.R.S. Office Armory Lockbox Code: RRR

Puzzle Walkthrough

At the Raccoon City Police Station, climb the ladder from reception upstairs to the Lounge. In the S.T.A.R.S. Office you'll find nostalgic callbacks: Rebecca's medical bag, Jill's beret, and Chris's jacket.

On Barry's desk, pick up [File 59/75] Barry's To-Do List. Learn that Captain Wesker borrowed a book from the Library and never returned it.

Wesker's office is a separate partition. You can see the old group photo of the S.T.A.R.S. team.

Open Wesker's desk drawer — it's empty at first. Wait a moment and [File 60/75] Library Card appears. Learn the borrowed book is "The Medicinal Value of Herbs."

Take the stairs to the Library upper floor. Find Wesker's borrowed book and discover Rebecca's photo inside. Wesker, how many photos of Rebecca did you hide?!

The first letters of three words on the back of Rebecca's photo form the code: RRR. Obtain [File 61/75] Note on Back of Photo.

Answer

The Locker Key is in the Command Room — climb the wooden crate to reach it up high.

Puzzle Walkthrough

Climb the ladder from reception upstairs to the Lounge. In the S.T.A.R.S. Office, you'll see nostalgic callbacks: Rebecca's medical bag, Jill's beret, and Chris's jacket.

At Chris's jacket, pick up [File 57/75] Treasure Hunt - Clue 1.

Enter the S.T.A.R.S. Office and look right — find [File 58/75] Treasure Hunt - Clue 2 in the cabinet.

Following both Treasure Hunt clues, go to the Command Room and climb the wooden crate to obtain the [Locker Key] up high.

Use the key to open the locker in the West Office to obtain the [S.T.A.R.S. Badge Pendant]. You'll also see the gift Chris prepared for Barry — Raccoon City Zoo tickets.
