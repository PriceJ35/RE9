RE9 Walkthrough Part 3 — Cemetery & Water Treatment Plant

Source: entertainment14.net / Ali213

Cemetery & Water Treatment Plant

Head straight ahead and take a right turn.

There is a Herb on the left side of the main path.

The open door on the right is the Save Room here.

On the table to the left inside the room, you will find the “Girl Eradication Operation” document.

The monitor here shows the captured Emily, currently lying on a bed asleep.

Before saving, you can store items you do not need in the Item Box. Right now the Access Card and Antique Coin are not needed, so feel free to stash them.

After saving, exit the room and head right. Open the map and make your way to the Confinement Area.

When we reach the confinement area corridor, a short cutscene plays. We try to wake Emily, but she's fast asleep and doesn't respond.

To open this cell door, we need to restore power to the machine ahead.

The leftmost cell door is open. Inside, you can find Scrap and blood.

Go to the machine and first interact with the manual on the wall: "How to Open the Cell Door." We now need to collect three plugs to restore power here.

Press M to open the map and see the plug locations.

First, hold F to remove this plug.

After removing the plug, the giant Abnormal Zombie spawns behind us — we should now call it "the Girl." Don't panic. Remember the open cell door on the right? Go inside and hide.

Turn off the flashlight inside. Listen for its footsteps to fade before moving. When it seems safe, peek out.

After it leaves, keep going forward and insert the plug to restore power.

After the iron door opens, there's Scrap on the table to the left.

On the table ahead, there's a "Mr. Raccoon" figurine and a "Technician's Note."

After getting both items, go out and pull out this plug.

Head toward the power room near the incinerator.

Insert the plug here.

There's a zombie inside.

Kill it, then enter the room on the right. There's a safe inside.

The safe combination is from the note Leon picked up earlier: →60 ←40 →80.

Inside are Ammo and the "Raccoon Tracking Map (Rose Mountain)." This item shows the locations of Mr. Raccoons on Rose Mountain.

Open the map and you'll see one in the lower right, but the current mission takes priority.

On the table on the other side, you can find the "Security Guard's Report."

After collecting the items, exit. The machine on the right requires a key, so we can't use it yet.

The plug on the left can be pulled out.

After getting this plug, we have two options.

The first option is to go back the way we came and get the plug at the entrance.

The second option is to crawl through the vent near the corpse.

After crawling through, there are Lockpicks on the floor to the left.

Pick up the tool and continue right. A zombie appears ahead, but "the Girl" drags it away.

Wait here until it goes up before moving. This animation only triggers when walking this way.

The door here can't be opened without power, but you can step on the crate to the left of the door to climb up. After exiting, go to the incinerator entrance to grab that plug.

After pulling the plug, zombies will attack from ahead.

Kill them, then return to the Save point.

After saving, go out and turn left. There's a socket on the left — insert the plug.

This socket is a bit misleading at first glance. It seems like we should go in and grab the items underwater, but the electrified floor prevents us.

Don't force your way through. The main objective is actually the room across, which are the two doors at the bottom of the map. (I missed this on my first playthrough, so I didn't explore inside. There's a "Mr. Raccoon" on the TV in the room.)

After scavenging the room, pull the plug out and head to the power room above.

After entering, push this wooden crate.

Press W to squeeze through the gap.

After squeezing through, there's a Herb on the right.

In the water on the floor to the left, you can find Lockpicks. (This is where the electricity was earlier.)

In the red toolbox on the right, you'll find a "Four-Sided Socket Wrench."

After getting the wrench, head back. On the way, you can see "the Girl" eating a corpse near the gap.

When you see it turn around and walk away, quickly squeeze through the gap.

Come over and pull the plug out.

After pulling it out, head toward the Save point. When you reach the Save point entrance, "the Girl" appears in front of us — immediately duck into the Save room on the right.

Save your game. When you hear silence, exit, turn right, and head toward the red light on the left.

Inside the door, you can find Scrap on the right.

There's a zombie blocking the path on the left.

Kill it, then interact with the control panel on the right. Use the wrench we picked up to open this metal box.

After opening the box, insert both plugs.

After restoring power, you'll notice "the Girl" is right behind us. Ignore it and head inside.

The iron plates here will collapse. Take the pipe path on the left.

Press CTRL to crouch and enter.

There's Scrap in the corner on the left.

After crawling out, grab the Herb outside.

Next, climb up the stairs on the left.

After climbing up, there's a blood pool here. Next to the corpse on the left, you can obtain the "Winch Key."

After getting the key, insert it into the winch on the left.

The winch will move, allowing us to enter the door it was blocking.

On the desk, you can find a new Fanny Pack.

On the left is the "Sanatorium Underground Facility Floor Plan."

The blueprint on the wall is also worth checking.

There's Ammo on the left.

After scavenging, go back the way we came. Leave the plug here — don't remove it.

While in the pipes, this creepy thing will suddenly appear again.

Still ignore it. Come out and pull out both plugs.

After pulling them out, head toward the objective. Both doors can be entered.

I'm not sure why I took the long way around toward the workshop. If you go that way, "the Girl" will ambush you when you crawl out of the pipe. (If you go toward the incinerator, she'll also jump down at this spot.)

Ignore it here too. Go directly to the right and interact with the winch. Insert the key — we have glorious invincibility frames.

Then quickly come over and insert the plug, or you'll get caught.

Once inside, we're temporarily safe. Don't touch this switch yet.

At this point, the body bags ahead are moving counter-clockwise. Follow the conveyor belt and let the body bags carry you in.

After getting through, zombies will drop from ahead. Deal with them — there's also Ammo on the right.

After clearing them, interact with this cart.

After pulling the cart over, a path opens up. Walk through here.

Come over and pull the lever to change the direction of the body bags.

Now follow this path inside. (The hanging zombies might grab you occasionally, but they won't deal damage.)

After walking through, more zombies will drop down.

Deal with them and keep pushing forward.

After getting through, there's a Herb in the left corner.

There's a Save point on the right.

After saving, come over and turn the central wheel.

The water level below will drop. Jump down — there are plenty of resources to scavenge nearby.

While scavenging, the nearby ladder retracts, and the meat grinder starts processing zombie corpses.

We need to keep backing up while fighting off zombies. Aim for the head, then use physical attacks to push them into the grinder.

After clearing, take the stairs on the right to go up.

Come up and pull out this plug.

Then head to the Save point across. Go through the pipe on the left.

We'll slide down the pipe to the blood pool.

Walk up the stairs — there's a Herb right in front of you.

If you're low on blood, you can find this switch nearby. Interact with it.

The blood pool is full of collectible blood.

If you don't need blood, go straight to the elevator nearby.

Now head back upstairs to retrieve the two plugs. (It's easier if you go through the incinerator.)

A confinement area door ahead has opened. Kill the zombie inside — there's an Empty Syringe on the bed you can pick up.

You can use it to craft either a Steroid or Stabilizer.

Continue retrieving plugs. Don't pull the one at the entrance yet — if you do, you won't be able to get back in.

Retrieve this one first.

While retrieving, "the Girl" will spawn in front of us again.

Quickly crawl back through the hole.

Once at the entrance, you can now pull this plug out.

Then go straight to open the cell door.

Insert all three plugs to successfully restore power.

Now we need to carry Emily to the elevator.

There will be many zombies blocking the way. If your positioning isn't great, getting bitten a few times isn't a big deal — just remember to heal.

At the elevator, the doors are blocked by debris. Rapidly press F to clear it.

After starting the elevator, "the Girl" will peek in from the top during the ride. Ignore it.

Interact with the button ahead — again, rapidly press F.

After interacting, a cutscene plays. We barely escape, and "the Girl" loses a hand.

Next, Emily hands over the "Star Crystal" to us.

At this point, "the Girl's" hand is marked on the map and can be collected. Don't forget to grab it.

You can go to the lobby to save first.

After saving, bring the Level 3 Access Wristband and enter the door on the left — the room where Emily was held. We're here to open the adjacent room.

There's a "Mr. Raccoon" next to the bed, and a doll on the right you can examine.

After collecting, bring Emily to open the main gate and insert the last stone.

However, there are still some locks on the map that can be opened. Feel free to explore if interested.

Carry Emily outside. There's an Antique Coin on the chair in the lower left corner of the courtyard.

There's a Herb on the fountain in the center.

You can find a Simple Knife in the water.

Then head toward the nearby helipad.

There's a pilot on the helicopter. He'll ask us to find the helicopter key while he takes care of Emily.

Now enter the residence on the right. On the desk, you can find the “Visitor Log.”

There's an Item Box on the right for storage. We probably don't need the extra space right now though.

Continue inside and interact with the bottle on the left first.

After interacting with the bottle, rotate its pattern to align the design.

Once aligned, press F to open it, then press A and D to extend the bottle.

Finally, you'll receive a “Double Helix Model.”

You can examine the items in the display case on the right.

The books on the bookshelf to the left are also worth reading.

On the desk, you'll find “Notes on Research Results.”

There's also an hourglass on the desk you can pick up.

You can interact with the hourglass. Remove the screws from both sides.

After removing them, you obtain the “Hourglass.”

The globe behind you can be examined.

The paintings and objects on the left wall are worth examining.

Then come to the center and interact with this machine.

Insert the “Double Helix Model” we just obtained.

After inserting it, a mechanism triggers and the room descends. On the desk in the adjacent room, there's “Spencer's Research Report Part 2.”

You can save nearby. After saving, take the stairs down.

After coming down the stairs, go left. There's a Medical Syringe in this drawer.

The central area doesn't have items, just things to examine. Walk further inside — on this stretcher, there's “Test Subject 170 Record.”

There's a map on the left wall.

Go up the stairs on the left. After climbing up, there's nothing on the left, so enter the door ahead.

Inside, on the table to the left, there's “Grace Ashcroft's Blood Analysis Report.”

Continue to the right. On the desk, you can find the “Helicopter Key.”

After getting the key, interact with the computer.

Press F to unlock the door. The locked door on the left will open.

There's a “Mr. Raccoon” on the table to the right.

Destroy it and enter the door. Inside, we see Victor on the phone with someone. As he leaves, he activates the self-destruct mechanism.

We need to get out immediately. When exiting, the room we came down in has already moved back up.

We can only go down the stairs now.

The area below is crawling with zombies. Grab the spray cans hanging on the walls to eliminate them.

Right-click to aim the spray can, left-click to spray. It's very effective against zombies.

When one can runs out, grab another from a nearby wall. There's more than enough to handle these zombies.

Keep going forward. There's a cave-in ahead — take the stairs up.

Just keep running up along the path.

Once you reach ground level, climb in through this window.

Return to the helipad and give the key to the pilot to prepare for takeoff.

Unsurprisingly, the pilot is attacked by zombies. The helicopter crash-lands and the pilot dies onboard.

Emily is also injured at this point.

The scene shifts back to Leon. The timeline aligns with Grace's crash — the helicopter just crashed outside the window.

Grace is carrying Emily and can't use her hands to fight zombies, so we need to provide support.

Press F to climb outside for a better vantage point.

Open the sniper scope. Press SHIFT to zoom in. Take out the three zombies at the iron gate. (Body shots are also one-hit kills.)

After killing those three, move forward to a new sniping position.

Grab the Ammo on the windowsill and protect Grace from this window.

A zombie will emerge from the bushes directly in front of Grace.

The second zombie appears from the right.

The third zombie appears from behind.

After killing these three zombies, Grace runs forward. Relocate to a new position — head inside.

Continue protecting her from the outside balcony. (Note: Don't shoot the propane tank yet.)

The first zombie appears directly ahead.

The second zombie also stands up from the front. The one lying in front of the door can be taken out early — that counts as the third.

The fourth zombie comes from the bushes behind.

After dealing with the zombie behind, Grace will try to break down the door. At this point, two zombies spawn near the fence on the left — take them both out.

One more zombie charges from behind. (Still don't shoot the propane tank.)

Next, two more zombies spawn on the right.

After dealing with them, a new Chef Zombie and a regular zombie spawn behind us. The propane tank upfront is meant for the Chef.

Wait until the Chef gets close, then shoot the propane tank.

Not done yet — another zombie approaches Grace from the left.

But it trips and falls.

This is the last one. After taking it out, Grace makes it through the door. Now head to the church — you can just jump down.

After dropping down, a group of zombies spawns ahead. Throw the propane tank and wait for the zombies to get close, then shoot it to take them all out.

Another restless zombie will block the path ahead. Take it out.

After entering the church, let Grace take Emily ahead first. We'll stay and deal with this mini-boss. The "tumors" on its body are the weak points — focus on shooting them.

You can also lure it near the propane tanks and blow them up.

Each time it takes heavy damage, you can use a physical attack to "finish" it. Each finisher removes one tumor. We need to destroy all its tumors.

Each time a tumor explodes, the nearby zombies revive once. Use grenades to deal with them.

There's Ammo on the stairs nearby, and a propane tank at the bottom. Lure the boss over and blow it up.

The axe on the ground downstairs can be used by pressing F to interact. Leon will automatically throw it at zombies — very straightforward.

On the table downstairs, there are Grenades and blood medicine Supplies.

After defeating it, walk toward the door to trigger a cutscene. We open the door and end this chapter.

The scene shifts back to Grace. We carry Emily underground. Follow the main path and enter the large door.

After entering, put Emily down. At this point, "the Girl" is chasing us from outside again.

On the nearby table, there are various Supplies and a Save point.

The notebook on the desk is the "Ceiling Operation Manual." We need to restore power again.

Save your game and prepare to head out. There's also a 100 blood bag on the crate behind you.

After exiting, try operating this device first. It'll play an animation showing insufficient power.

The game will prompt you to open the map and check the power system locations. Don't go toward the stairs behind you — follow the red arrow ahead.

At the position ahead, there's a staircase going down.

Keep going down. You'll find Ammo on this crate.

Walk a few steps forward and you'll see "the Girl" right in front of us.

If she hasn't spotted us, just go left. (If she has, hit her with a Requiem shot.)

Keep walking left. In the left corner, you can find a Medical Syringe.

If "the Girl" is chasing you, use this light against her. Then continue left.

Come over and pull this control lever. However, this lever is faulty and hard to pull.

Simply holding F won't be fast enough. First, shoot "the Girl" dropping down from above on the left with Requiem.

Once it enters a staggered state, immediately start holding F to pull the lever.

After succeeding, the power restarts successfully. She'll be hit by light and then flee.

There's also a Molotov Cocktail on the table behind us that can be used to temporarily deal with her.

There are two more objectives. The game forces us to go to the one on the left first.

Because "the Girl" will jump down to block our path halfway.

Follow the map to this control room. The switch doesn't even have a lever.

If she gets close, hit her with another Requiem shot.

Enter the door on the left.

On the shelf, you can find a "Crank Lever."

After picking it up, she'll walk toward you. Use the Molotov Cocktail or Requiem to drive her back. (Throw the Molotov carefully — you can burn yourself.)

Exit quickly and interact with the lever ahead to attach the crank.

After powering up, she escapes again.

After powering up, exit and go left.

Keep going left.

On the blue barrel to the left, there's a Requiem Ammo round.

After picking it up, go right. You'll see a light here. Wait for her to approach, then turn it on to drive her back.

After driving her back, go pull the lever ahead. This one finally works without issues.

With all three power sources restored, head to the elevator to go up.

Take the elevator back upstairs. After a few steps, "the Girl" climbs up from below again. Ignore her and activate the mechanism on the left.

Force open the ceiling. The light will shine on her. Finish her off with a Requiem shot.

After the kill, she melts into a puddle of sludge. Finally dead.

Next, go find Emily. Unfortunately, it's too late — Emily has died. The deceased Emily transforms into a monster. We control Leon to kill it. (Even choosing not to shoot doesn't help — it seems to be a forced kill.)

After killing the monster Emily became, Grace blames herself.

Grace refuses to leave with Leon. On her own, she encounters Victor. A mysterious man also appears. Grace follows them away.
