RE9 Walkthrough Part 5 — Tyrant Boss & Plant 43

Source: entertainment14.net / Ali213

Exploration & Raccoon City Police Station

Head over here and open the door.

Once the door opens, climb the stairs up.

Take out the Zombies on the ground. The crate nearby has plenty of Supplies.

Grab the loot and head back to the street.

Open the map again — we only need one last part. Head back to camp, get ready to enter the logistics warehouse, and take the elevator on the left side of camp straight up.

After riding the elevator up, go down through the entrance on the right, then climb the stairs to reach the fuel machine. Walk up to it, inject fuel, and start it up.

This opens the door on the left. Go through and Scavenge for Supplies.

There is a rope here you can use to cross to the other side.

After crossing, head inside. You will see a wooden door — the boards blocking it can be broken with melee attacks or firearms.

Inside you will find a Herb.

Grab it and turn back. You can walk out along the scaffolding here.

Follow the scaffolding and look up to shoot the hanging crate.

The crate contains 4,200 in cash.

After grabbing the cash, go down, then come up the stairs next to the door you opened earlier. The room on the right has a Mr. Raccoon. There are other Supplies in the room as well.

Collect everything and exit. Head toward the elevator — there is a path you can enter along the way.

Walk to this spot and break the two wooden boards.

After that, head this way to cross to the other side.

Grab the Supplies on the left, then shoot the boards on the right.

Once the boards are gone, press F to jump across.

After two consecutive jumps, shoot the boards below.

And the boards above as well.

The path is now clear. Drop down.

After dropping down, keep moving forward. Rapidly press F to pry open the elevator doors ahead.

Go down after opening the doors. As you climb up, wooden crates will slide down toward you — first from the right, then from the left.

Once at the top, slide down on the right side.

Step onto the glass and break the boards on the left.

After opening the door, there will be Zombies on the glass panels ahead. Shoot the glass out from under them to make them fall.

There are several more on the right. Some of them are positioned right in your path, so watch out.

You can safely stand on the colored panels.

Break the glass panel on the left to reveal a path upward.

After climbing up, a swarm of Zombies spawns ahead. Just toss a grenade right into them.

Clear them all out and keep moving forward. Open the door ahead.

Behind the door is a bunch of Supplies.

Open the crate on the left to get the final part, then assemble it.

With all parts collected, we need to find a way back. The Save point is on the left — grab the Supplies and do any upgrades.

Save and exit, then cross the bridge.

Coming out of the room on the other side, shoot the stairs on the left — there are Supplies up there.

After getting the Supplies, walk over and Interact. You will need to use the binoculars.

Take a look at the opposite side, then exit.

From here, use the sniper rifle to pick off the Zombies on the platform across from you. Eliminate the ones visible from the front, then head over to that platform.

Climb the stairs and you will find another group of Zombies. A grenade is recommended — the mutated ones are tough to fight.

At the very top, yet another wave of Zombies spawns. Lure them down and blow up the gas canister near the stairwell to take them out.

This wooden crate contains a Herb. There is a shortcut here — climb up from the left side of the crate to skip the stairs.

After climbing up from the left of the crate, we can drop down from here.

If you still want cash, take the stairs on the right — there are a few more Zombies over there.

If you do not need the money, just slide down.

After sliding down, Interact with the rotary wheel.

Go down the stairs on the right to reach a fork. The crate on the left has a grenade, the door on the left leads to a Save point, and the right path is the main route.

Save and head down the main path. There is a Herb along the way.

You can ambush the Zombie through the window on the left.

Walk forward a few steps, equip the sniper, and land some headshots from this vantage point (you can remove the scope for better aim).

After dealing with the two below, take a few steps to the right. New Zombies will spawn — prioritize the one on the second floor.

After killing it, a mutated Zombie appears on the right. Take it down.

Now you can advance. From this spot, kill the Zombie playing dead in the corner.

Grab the Ammo ahead after killing it.

Stand here and observe the Zombies above. One of them will throw objects at you, so dodge accordingly.

After it finishes throwing, it will walk down to the left. Wait until it is near the gas canister and blow it up to take out a bunch of Zombies at once.

If any survive, finish them off with a grenade or gunfire.

This position is still quite safe.

Once everything is dead, move upward. There is another gas canister ahead — shoot it preemptively.

Keep moving forward, open the door, and head inside. There are lots of Supplies here.

After collecting the Supplies, turn the rotary wheel.

Jump straight down and take the “elevator.”

During the elevator ride, many Zombies will appear on the opposite side.

You can toss grenades straight through the windows at them.

Some will inevitably survive — finish those off with your gun.

Mutated Zombies may even drop down. Fire a few shots to deal with them.

When you reach the bottom, a Cutscene triggers automatically and you jump down.

Another large group of Zombies is waiting in ambush below. If you want the score, grab the gas canister on the left and blow them up. If not, just run to the right.

Jump across directly so the Zombies will not chase you.

After exiting the Sewers, you will be at the mission objective entrance. Blow it open.

Once the door is blown open, you will ride a motorcycle. At first, just steer left and right.

After driving for a while, Victor shows up with his goons to attack. You can shoot back while riding.

Up ahead, you will need to keep shooting gas canisters to blast open the road.

You can also shoot Victor when he is alongside you.

Finally, on the platform a Cutscene plays. Aim and shoot. Victor falls, and after a bit of taunting, you leave.

We have now officially arrived in the heart of Raccoon City.

After dismounting the motorcycle, head to the ruins on the left first.

There is a Mr. Raccoon in the ruins.

Grab it and keep heading inside until you reach the familiar Police Station.

Once inside, both doors on the left and right require tools to open.

Walk forward first. A Cutscene plays and we discover Grace and Zeno are here too.

After the Cutscene, the crate behind you has a Raccoon City Police Station Vicinity Investigation Report.

There is an old mechanism on the stairs.

Then head back down to save and upgrade.

After saving, enter through the small door on the right.

On the desk you will find Top Secret Operation documents. The nameplates can also be examined.

The cabinet on the left requires a key to open.

Exit through the door on the right. You can skip the door here for now — go left first.

A Zombie will come around the corner of the corridor. Deal with it.

There is a body on the right you can Investigate.

After investigating, turn around and enter the door. Pick up the Ammo on the floor and shoot the stairs above.

After climbing up, you can examine the statue here.

Next, take the door straight ahead. The door on the right leads to the Library — we will get to that later.

The door on the right side of the corridor is accessible.

Inside, the cabinet on the right contains a photo — this is a puzzle clue.

There are two objects on the left you can Interact with.

Check the coat pocket for the second puzzle clue.

On the right wall there is a Medical Kit you can examine.

The room on the left has a photo you can examine.

Pay attention to this drawer. It appears empty when you first open it, but after a moment a piece of paper slides out.

This is a Library Card. If you do not pick this up first, the books in the Library cannot be examined.

On the far left desk is Barry’s To-Do List.

The room on the left has a safe that requires a combination. The clue for the code is in the Library we skipped earlier.

Retrace your steps to the Library. Upon entering, you will find a Jack on the floor.

Go to the back of this bookshelf and interact with the stack of books on the bottom. Inside is a Mr. Raccoon.

Now head up the stairs.

Interact with the book on this bookshelf.

Open it to reveal the safe combination.

Go back and open the safe. Enter the code RRR.

Inside the safe is the Ambition Charm. It gives your Ammo unlimited penetration and also boosts the power of the last round in a magazine.

After getting the charm, continue up the Library stairs. You can climb right over the bookshelves here.

Enter the door on the right. Drop down to collect Ammo and you will loop back to the main hall.

Now back in the main hall, go through this door again. We are going to get the key for the puzzle clue we found earlier.

A Zombie will spawn on the left side of the corridor. Deal with it.

After taking care of it, enter the door on the right. There is a Mr. Raccoon visible through the skylight on the left — shoot it to collect it.

The blackboard has an Incident Log you can read.

Then climb the crates on the right.

The key is right next to the TV above.

After picking up the key, an easter egg may randomly spawn on the right. Shooting it plays a sound but does not seem to do anything else (no achievement either).

Now head back to the office we were in earlier.

Open the cabinet here.

Inside the cabinet you will find the S.T.A.R.S. Badge Charm.

This charm can be attached to SMGs and Rifles. It boosts damage but increases weapon recoil.

You also receive the puzzle reward: two unused zoo tickets.

Once you have explored everything here, go open the iron door on the other side.

There is nothing along the way — just keep walking. A Cutscene triggers showing Grace and Zeno talking. When the conversation ends, the perspective switches.

The next perspective is quite different — you play as the little girl Chloe.

After waking up, you can Interact with the bed and the calendar nearby.

Then leave the room. Walking to the doorway triggers a Cutscene.

Inside, a group of children has killed a researcher. They spot you — hurry back to your room and hide.

Return to the room and crawl under the bed.

The girls will check each bed one by one. Wait until they finish checking yours and leave, then crawl out.

Go to the room on the left. You can examine the body.

Head left and crouch-walk through.

Open the door and go down the stairs. When you hear noise below, hide under this desk.

Wait for the girls to move away, then head for the door ahead.

If you move quickly, you may still see a girl standing by the door at the corner. Back up and wait for her to leave.

When the coast is clear, keep going inside and turn on the desk lamp on the right.

There is a key on the left wall. Pull out the cabinet below and climb up onto it.

After grabbing the key, walk forward a few steps. A girl will drop down ahead.

Immediately turn around and hide behind the crates here.

Stay crouched here. It looks like you will be discovered, but do not worry — it is just a Cutscene. They get drawn away by another sound.

Once they leave, go open the basement door ahead.

Head into the basement and you will arrive at a chapel. Keep walking straight, then activate the mechanism. Once it is activated, the lamp on the left lights up.

Walk toward the light. Open the coffin here to obtain a Body Management Tag.

Then go to the left and pull out this coffin.

Use the coffin as a platform and climb all the way up.

Inside, you obtain the Fire Hook.

Open the door and exit. Lots of little girls suddenly appear nearby.

Ignore them. Go to the mechanism from earlier and use the Fire Hook to pull down the stairs.

Climb up to what looks like a high-tech facility. The green door can be opened.

Enter and find the Series 60 Experiment Results on the desk.

On the other desk, find the New Researcher’s Notebook.

The monitor can be examined.

Finish investigating here and go through the other door. The hallway has many small dolls resembling infants.

Walk forward, enter the room, and Interact with the slide projector.

After going through the slides, a Cutscene plays. Chloe is captured by a researcher, and her perspective ends here.

The perspective switches back to Leon at the police station. Grace faints from grief after learning the truth about herself and the little girls. Zeno carries her away, leaving the Tyrant to keep us company.

We need to escape the police station. Walk forward a few steps and the Tyrant bursts in from the left, blocking the path.

Turn around and run — head to the right.

At the corridor, turn left and go up the stairs.

After reaching the top, keep going along the left passage. Partway through, the wall on the right explodes — turn back.

Turn back and enter this room.

The Tyrant breaks through the wall again.

Hit him with a Requiem round to knock him down (you cannot kill him). Head through the large hole on the left.

Keep walking. Once you are outside, there is a brief breather. The game prompts you to open the map and prepare to head to the Orphanage.

Before moving on, turn back and grab the City Brochure on the chair.

Set out for the Orphanage. The wall ahead has been destroyed, opening up a new path. Move forward and go left first.

There is an underground garage here. Head down. A Zombie is playing dead behind the car on the right — kill it first.

Another one is hiding in the corner on the left.

There are Supplies nearby.

After scavenging the left side, go to the area with light. Knock the Zombie out of the car and finish it off.

On the ground between the two cars on the right is a Cute Bear Charm.

This charm boosts medium-range damage.

There is 3,700 in cash on this crate.

The back of the truck contains a Scope and Supplies.

Grab everything and head back to the surface. Go to the other side.

Walk forward a bit more and a flashback Cutscene plays. After it ends, you are inside a room. Pick up the more powerful Shotgun from the shelf.

You can walk ahead — the Save point is right in front.

Save your game. The car on the right has a note (Deluxe Edition exclusive — this guide marks the location, since the author does not have it. The notes must be read in order).

Then walk back to the underground garage. There is a note in the corner by the car at the entrance.

After grabbing it, head near the small room — there is another alley here.

The shoes on the ground can be examined.

Look up at the window for a Mr. Raccoon, and check the right side for Supplies.

Keep walking left. There is another note on the ground here.

Finally, at this spot you can find a Badge (everything except the Raccoon is Deluxe Edition content).

Once you have collected everything, save, upgrade, and move forward.

After a Cutscene, the Tyrant boss fight begins. Parrying still works — the axe is absolutely the best weapon in the game.

The Tyrant’s weak point is the heart in the center. Shoot it whenever you get the chance. After dealing enough damage, you can close in and deal massive damage with the axe.

The fight is not too difficult. At most you will use a few healing items to take him down.

After defeating the Tyrant, there is Ammo on the left.

There is more Ammo next to the car behind you, and a Syringe inside the car.

The playground next door has Ammo as well. Open the door on the right to loop back and Save.

The doll head in the middle of the playground can be kicked into the basketball hoop for a small easter egg.
