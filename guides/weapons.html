RE9 Weapons Guide — All Weapon Locations

Source: entertainment14.net / Ali213 — Translated to English

S&S M232 Handgun

Starting weapon. After the story cutscene, you arrive on the streets of Sawarak City. Open your inventory to find the [Weapon] S&S M232 Handgun.

Getting the S&S M232 Handgun a Second Time

Location: Sanatorium — Recreation Room.

At the Sanatorium bar counter, recover your [Weapon] S&S M232.

Alligator Handgun + Requiem Revolver + Axe

Leon's starting weapons. When the story first switches to Leon, his inventory includes the [Weapon] Alligator Handgun, [Weapon] Requiem Revolver, and [Weapon] Axe.

When Leon is grabbed by a zombie, follow the on-screen prompt to counter with axe. You can also use the axe for stealth kills or finishers.

When you stun a zombie, watch for the prompt — you can use a physical attack to deal damage.

B934 Handgun

Located on the west side of the first floor of the Sanatorium. Head north to the dining room to find the [Weapon] B934 Handgun.

You don't have to fight the zombie eating at the dining table. Go around to the other side — there's an empty bottle on the cabinet. You can come back for it after picking up the handgun ammo.
