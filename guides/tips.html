RE9 Essential Tips — 12 Survival Tips

Source: entertainment14.net / Ali213 — Translated to English

1. Lifesaver move "Quick Turn" — Controls: Keyboard X / Left stick back + ◦ / Left stick back + B. When sudden danger approaches, a quick turn and retreat is your best bet.

2. When Leon is grabbed by a zombie, follow the on-screen prompt to counter with axe. You can also use the axe for stealth kills or finishers.

3. When you stun a zombie, watch for the prompt — you can use a physical attack to deal damage.

4. Zombies sometimes drop weapons. If there are other zombies nearby, follow the prompt to throw these weapons at them.

5. While exploring the map, many vehicles have supplies in the trunk — make sure to search thoroughly.

6. When you first wake up in Room 203 of the Sanatorium, there are interactive items in the room. Pay special attention to the light switch — keep the lights on, as they will be very useful later.

7. Similarly, use the key to open Room 201. The typewriter is for saving, there's an empty bottle in the cabinet, and it's best to keep the room lights on.

These two tips above will come in handy when dealing with monsters later on.

8. To reach boxes on high shelves you must use a push cart. But be careful — dragging the cart makes noise that will attract monsters.

9. When a monster hasn't spotted you, throw an empty bottle to distract it. However, since empty bottles are used later to craft Molotov Cocktails and Sulfuric Acid Bottles, try to use them sparingly.

10. Be careful — a chainsaw dropped by a zombie will keep running on the ground for a while and can hurt you if you get close. Wait for it to stop, then pick it up for combat. Also try to keep other zombies away from the chainsaw, or they'll pick it up and attack you. After eliminating zombies you can also use the chainsaw to open doors.

11. Grace's physical attack can only knock zombies down — follow up by shooting them in the head while they're on the ground.

12. Use a Hemolytic Syringe to perform a stealth kill from behind a zombie, or instantly kill a stunned zombie. Zombies that play dead on the ground will eventually revive — to prevent this, use a Hemolytic Syringe to finish them off for good.
