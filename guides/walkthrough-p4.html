RE9 Walkthrough Part 4 — NF Building & Police Station

Source: entertainment14.net / Ali213

NF Building & Police Station

In this chapter, Leon returns to his old stomping grounds — Raccoon City.

Once you gain control of Leon, there's a “Newspaper from the Raccoon City Incident” on the ground to the right.

On the iron fence to the left, there's a missing person notice you can read.

Next to the car behind the fence, you'll find some Ammo.

On the wall at the dead end to the right, there's an eviction notice to check.

Open the map and head toward the objective marker.

The main path we need to take is through here. The container on the right can't be opened yet, so ignore it for now.

Enter the hotel. Next to the globe on the right, there's some Scrap.

Behind the bar counter on the left, there's a Medical Syringe.

On the floor to the left of the door, there's Ammo.

After Scavenging everything, push the door and interact with it, then move forward.

After going outside, head left. When you reach the doorway, a giant spider will attack you.

Now it's time to fight the spider. Grab the Ammo on the left first.

The spider webs ahead can be cut with a left mouse click.

About halfway through, it will burst through the wall. This is when you can see its weak point — its rear end.

Keep backing up while shooting it. It will eventually force you into a room.

Take the opportunity to fire a few shots at its rear, then move deeper inside. There's a door here — go through it.

Keep heading upstairs. There are lots of spider webs along the way — cut through all of them. You'll find Ammo on the right.

The room ahead has many small spiders. You can shoot them one by one or use explosives.

You can fake out the window ahead, otherwise you'll get hit.

Then climb the stairs.

Once you're up, the real fight with the giant spider begins. There are plenty of Supplies nearby.

Try to circle behind it and shoot its rear end.

After damaging it enough, it will hide. Go to the next room and climb the stairs.

The spider will reappear. Wait until it gets close, then shoot the gas canister to blow it up.

There are gas canisters upstairs too. You can pick one up and throw it at its face, or lure it close and detonate it.

Keep moving forward. When you reach this corner, a cutscene triggers — the spider grabs you again.

Mash the F key to break free.

After breaking free, it appears in front of you. This time it sprays venom and small spiders from its rear. Use the wall on the right as cover.

Deal with the small spiders — melee attacks work well here.

After a while it crawls away again. Continue up the stairs.

When you reach the top, it will suddenly ambush you from behind. One more shot should finish it off.

After a short cutscene, you arrive underground.

Go up the steps nearby. There's a staircase here you can climb.

Once up, walk forward and push through the revolving door.

Unlock the door and head outside.

Walk up to the main gate for a short cutscene. This door needs explosives to blast open, but we're missing a detonator.

The warning sign on the left can be inspected.

Head left first — there's a camp site here.

On the table inside the tent, there's “Orders to the Engineering Unit.”

Other items need power to access. The generator is right outside — go turn it on.

After restoring power, there's another cutscene. Our computer connection is being monitored by Victor. The other man on the plane is named Chino, and he tells Victor to come deal with us.

After the cutscene, interact with the Item Box on the left to obtain the “Combat Log Recorder.”

These points can be used to buy Weapons, items, or upgrade Weapons. Points are earned by killing Zombies.

You don't have points yet, so let's continue the mission. There are Supplies in the crate outside the tent.

After grabbing those, come operate the mechanism on the left.

The iron gate will rise just a little — crouch to get under it.

Once inside, at the corner, there's a door on the left.

You can pick up a Grenade from the table.

The cabinet also contains some Supplies.

After Scavenging, head deeper inside. A large horde of Zombies will suddenly emerge in the middle area — consider tossing a Grenade.

After clearing this wave, more will appear from the other side.

Once they're all dealt with, flip this switch.

Look up and to the left — shoot the hanging ring above.

After the crate lowers, climb up. Zombies will also appear up here.

Standing on this platform, you can also shoot the ring up high — there's Ammo and Supplies on the other side.

You can also jump down from here, but it's just a few rounds of Ammo and some Zombies — skipping it is fine.

In the corner by the shelves on the right, there's an Herb.

At the very top, shoot the ring to lower the crate so you can walk across. Enter the door on the right.

There's a Zombie right behind the door — take it out.

Find the “Cedar Creek Apartment Key” on the wall.

Continue inside to the balcony. Take the stairs next to the door.

The fuel generator upstairs can't be started yet — we need to find fuel first.

Then enter the door on the right. Inside there are more stairs — climb up.

After climbing up, turn right. There's an Herb on this crate.

Now go left. Find the Distributor inside the yellow crate.

After picking it up, use the telescope to survey the surroundings. When you spot an objective marker, press F to inspect it.

The first one is straight ahead.

The second one is to the left.

The third one is up high behind us.

After checking all three locations, take the elevator next to you down.

Take the elevator back down to the camp. The Zombies we killed are worth about 9,000 points — enough to buy gear (the author bought all available Supplies and an SMG).

After shopping, save your game, then open the back door next to the camp.

The door is right next to the elevator.

After opening the door, shoot the supply crate up high — it contains a Grenade.

Walk over and shoot the ladder to lower it. There are Supplies up top.

After grabbing them, enter the door downstairs. There are Zombies in the hallway — sneak up for a Stealth Kill.

This Zombie drops 1,200 points.

After killing it, enter the door on the left. A “Mr. Raccoon” is visible as soon as you open the door.

Grab the nearby Supplies as well.

After Scavenging, keep going. Through the next door, you'll see a Zombie pounding on an iron gate in the water — Stealth Kill it.

After killing it, read the nearby note “Battery Storage Point” and interact with the machine.

After interacting, a new objective appears. Open the map — it's marked as a black diamond icon.

Turn around first — there are stairs right behind you. Climb up.

Once up, jump straight down into the water below.

The Zombies here have poor vision and hearing — you can Stealth Kill all of them.

Several containers can be opened for Scavenging.

One container has Zombie sounds coming from inside. When you open it, sure enough there's one — shoot the gas canister inside to instantly take care of it.

The Zombies near the entrance are clustered together — a Grenade works wonders here.

After clearing them, you can explore the area for additional Supplies.

Continue the mission — enter the door and grab the Battery from the wall.

On the table to the right, there's a “Gall” SMG. If you don't pick it up now, it will appear in the shop next chapter, but its stats aren't as good as the one you can buy.

After getting the Battery, take the stairs nearby.

Up here there's a narrow path with Zombies on it.

Walk to the end and jump down. This is where the Zombie was pounding on the iron gate earlier.

You can insert the Battery here first.

Then get out of the water and go through this door.

There are Supplies on the shelves in this room. Grab them, then jump down from here.

There's an Herb in the corridor below.

Keep going deeper until you reach a brightly lit area. Head downward.

After coming down, turn around — there are Supplies and a Hand Crank here.

After picking them up, Zombies spawn in front of you. Deal with them.

Once cleared, insert the Hand Crank and hold F to turn it.

A tumor monster appears midway — just like before, shoot the tumors.

When smaller Zombies spawn, back up while shooting.

There are gas canisters on the steps behind you — make use of them. Grab the Herb on the right too.

After clearing everything, remember to Scavenge the items dropped by the Zombies.

After Scavenging, keep cranking the wheel all the way to the end.

Once fully cranked, head up and you can go straight across.

Find the second Battery on the room's wall.

Then exit through the iron door on the left.

Follow the path all the way back to the water pool and insert the second Battery.

The iron door on the left will open.

Once on the surface, head left first. There's an Herb in this corner.

Approach the group of bodies to trigger a cutscene.

After the cutscene, obtain the “BSAA Container Key” from the soldier. This key opens the containers we couldn't access earlier.

The container on the left has Supplies inside.

On the crate, you can pick up the “Supply Manifest.”

The building on the left can be visited later — going there now won't advance the story.

There's a mechanism nearby — go pull it.

After pulling it, the crane above drops down, creating a path back to the camp.

Don't head to camp yet. Turn around and you'll see a bus nearby.

At the front of the bus, there's a “Mr. Raccoon.”

After collecting it, walk along the left side of the bus hugging the wall. You'll see a ladder you can knock down — shoot it.

After climbing up, there are Supplies straight ahead.

Grab them, then follow the path on the right and jump down.

After dropping down, Stealth Kill the Zombies here. There are some Supplies nearby to Scavenge.

The barrel by the iron gate straight ahead contains the fuel we need, but we don't have a fuel container yet.

Head through the door on the right first.

Inside, you can spot a “Mr. Raccoon” on the shelves.

There's Scrap on the shelf to the left.

The Zombie around the corner will rise and attack — take it out first.

Keep going deeper. There's Ammo in the cabinet on the left.

Find the “Fuel Can” outside the door.

After getting the Fuel Can, head back to fill it with fuel.

On the way back, Zombies will burst out from behind the crates — there are two of them.

After dealing with them, don't grab the chainsaw on the ground yet — draw your gun first. There's a self-destructing Zombie outside. Step out and shoot the gas canister to detonate it (otherwise you won't be able to react in time).

Then go back and grab the chainsaw.

There are several waves of Zombies here. You can also “blade clash” with the chainsaw Zombie that appears later. If you're struggling, just toss a few Grenades.

After clearing everything, Scavenge the resources on the ground — you'll earn a decent amount of points.

After Scavenging, come fill up on fuel.

With fuel in hand, return to where you found the Fuel Can and pour fuel into the fuel machine.

After refueling, the door on the right can be opened.

After going outside, open the map — you'll see a container marked on it. Go there first. It contains a Sniper Rifle (if your inventory is full, you can go back to camp and sell your current one first).

Follow the map to find the red container. You can climb on top of it.

Use the key to open the container — there are lots of Zombies inside. But there's a gas canister right behind you. Throw it in and detonate it.

A spear-wielding Zombie might survive the blast — finish it off with a few rounds of Ammo.

After killing it, go inside and pick up the “Sharpshooter 1A.”

With the gun in hand, come open this door to grab the collectibles inside.

Go to the door and inject fuel.

Inside, head up the stairs.

Look up and shoot the crate above.

There are plenty of Supplies on the left.

Then go back down the stairs, crouch and grab the Supplies that dropped.

After grabbing them, move forward. Take out the Zombie in front — you can Stealth Kill one of them.

But more Zombies will soon surround you — use the gas canisters nearby to blow them up.

After eliminating them all, go down this staircase.

At the end of the pipe on the right, you can collect 3,700 points.

At the end of the pipe on the left, there's a door.

Open the door and jump down.

There are Supplies on both sides — Scavenge them.

After grabbing them, walk forward to the subway car. There's Ammo on the right.

On the left is the subway train, with Zombies on board.

Continue into the darkness ahead — you'll see another train car.

Enter the car and go left. White small spiders will appear ahead. Kill them, then grab the “Mr. Raccoon” on the right.

After collecting everything, exit through the subway door on the right. You'll find yourself back where the giant spider died.

Take the stairs up to return to where we first arrived.

New Zombies will respawn here, including ones that lunge at your face.

After exiting the subway, our goal this time is to open the container here.

New Zombies will appear near the container.

After clearing them, open the container to get the “BSAA Emblem Pendant.” This pendant increases shooting power when no scope is attached.

And 8,600 points.

After getting the container's contents, you can go back to upgrade Supplies first, or go straight in to grab the “Crank Handle.”

That's where the soldiers died.

After entering the door, look right and jump out through this window.

Look up and shoot the crate above — it contains Requiem Ammo.

After grabbing it, explore the first floor. There are lots of Supplies here.

There's a Green Herb on the right.

The door on the right leads directly to the apartment, near the camp. If you want to spend points on upgrades, you can go through here.

If you're skipping the camp, come back and take the first-floor stairs up.

On the first-floor balcony outside, there's a door that requires a Crank Handle to open.

Go through the door on the right. A Zombie inside will stand up — kill it, then jump down to the right.

After dropping down, deal with the Zombie in the corner. Aim for the head.

More Zombies will drop from above.

After clearing them all, Scavenge the nearby Supplies and pull the lever to open the door.

After opening the door, you obtain the “Rusty Crank Handle.”

After opening this door, head upstairs. A self-destructing Zombie will rush out at this corner — detonate it early.

Go to the balcony and open this door.

After climbing the stairs, grab the Ammo from the crate, then enter the door on the right.

A “Mr. Raccoon” is hidden in this corner.

After collecting it, go back out and continue upward.

Before going up, get your Sniper Rifle ready.

At this position, the Zombie in front of you will take a tracking missile from their own allies.

Take out your Sniper Rifle and snipe the Zombie on the building across from you.

After the kill, duck into the room on the left to dodge incoming tracking missiles.

Inside the room, there's a chemical agent on the table you can pick up.

After grabbing it, use the gap in the wall on the left to take out a Zombie lying on the ground — it takes two shots.

After killing it, wait for the next missile to pass, then run to the room on the other side. There's another Zombie around the corner — it might get killed by a missile, but if not, finish it off yourself.

During the gap between missiles, take these stairs up.

Once up the stairs, prioritize taking out the missile Zombie on the left.

Then duck into the small room nearby to dodge the next missile (this room can only withstand one hit).

Take out your Sniper Rifle, look up and to the right, and take down that Zombie.

After taking it out, turn around and climb these stairs. Switch to a different gun before going up.

At the top, there's a self-destructing Zombie in front of you. Run to the right and blow it up, letting it take out the missile Zombie with it.

Next, use the missiles to take out the Zombies below. Another one will respawn on the right shortly. After killing them all, head to the other side, following the red arrow.

You can also Scavenge the nearby Supplies first.

After Scavenging, come this way and go up the stairs.

At the top, open the crate to get the “Engineer's Note.”

In the crate on the left, pick up the “Broken Signal Receiver.”

At this point, a “Find the Repair Toolbox” mission appears. The toolbox is actually inside the container next to our camp — you can go get it anytime.

There's also a motorcycle inside.

The Repair Toolbox.

Now we're only missing one more part.

More Zombies will spawn on the way back. You can return upstairs and keep using the missiles to take them out.

There's more than one wave. When the music stops, that means they're all gone.

After coming down, shoot the skylight crate nearby, then take the stairs down.

Zombies will respawn on the stairs. Don't waste the gas canister on the first Zombie.

Save the gas canister for the “Heavy Soldier” Zombie.

After clearing them, grab the Grenade in the stairway corner.

Jump down to the ground. In the middle is the yellow crate we shot down earlier — it contains 5,100 points.

Now open the map. Besides the final yellow objective, there's also a red door we haven't Explored yet.

This door requires going back to where we first surfaced (where the Zombie was pounding on the iron gate in the water). Enter through the main gate there.

After coming down, open the map and you'll see a door that previously needed a Crank Handle. We can now go open it.
